BuySafe Bonded

Overview

buySAFE is a Zoovy partner that offers bonding on items sold through your Zoovy store. To learn more about our partnership with buysafe, please visit www.zoovy.com/buysafe. To sign up visit http://www.buysafe.com/offerZoovy.

Enabling buySAFE

Once you have been approved by buySAFE to become a bonded seller, you will be provided with a Public Hash as well as individual domain tokens. If you do not have this information, contact your buySAFE account manager.
Enabling buySAFE
  • Log in to your account at www.zoovy.com
  • Click on the Setup tab, then Analytics & Plugins
  • Click on the buySAFE Website Bonding link
  • Click on Manual Setup
  • If Prompted, select 'production' for the environment
  • Enter your Public Hash
  • For each of the domains listed that you want to provide bonded transactions for, enter the domain token. Each domain should have a unique token
  • Save your changes
  • Look at your website to make sure buySAFE branding is appearing in footer
    • Once you have enabled buySAFE
    Once you have enabled your tokens, you should go through checkout (and purchase bonding!) and make sure your cart, invoices and emails are all functioning properly and displaying the proper bonding information.

    Additional buySAFE branding

    Once you have enabled your domain tokens and public hash, you can turn on additional buySAFE branding through the sidebar feature.
    Enabling additional buySAFE branding
  • Log in to your account at www.zoovy.com
  • Click on the Setup tab
  • Click on the Site Builder link
  • Click on the Sidebar/Header link, under the customize header on the left
  • Paste in the buySAFE premium seal html in the 'buysafe seal html' text area.
  • Save your changes
  • Look at your website to make sure buySAFE branding is appearing in the sidebar and not disturbing the aesthetic of your store
    • If there are any issues with the BuySafe signup, the BuySafe contact information is customersupport@buysafe.com or (888) 926-6333.
    Some of the buySAFE branding images are very wide. Putting this html into your sidebar will likely break the aesthetic of your store. It is recommended that you choose one of the more narrow seals to avoid this.
    Only the premium seal code will work in conjunction with the sidebar feature.

    To be fully Buysafe compliant

    To be fully compliant with Buysafe you need to be using a newer email template (specifically one with order contents formatted by a CART element) so it can include the buysafe line item content, footer message, a buysafe line after the tax and a buysafe bond in the total.
    All templates designed since Jan 1st. 2007 are compliant.
    Zoovy graphics department will be able to assist you further with the creation of the custom email template for Buysafe.

    Buysafe and Partitions

    When enabling Buysafe bonding, the selected configuration will be applied to all sites on a specified Partition. Either all, or none, of the sites on a Partition will be Buysafe bonded.
    Sites on the same Partition cannot be set independently in regarding this feature.

    How do buyers cancel Buysafe bonds?

    Before the buyer checks out they can uncheck the box in the cart. Or after the sale BuySafe sends the buyer an email which includes a link to cancel the bond.
