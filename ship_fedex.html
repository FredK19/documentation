FedEx

Set Up

Zoovy supports all popular FedEx services. There's not a lot of configuration that can go on except for the handling charge. The handling charge can be either a fixed price like $1.00 or a variable +%10, which would add ten percent to the quoted shipping rates. Some people use the handling charge to accommodate the weight of the boxes and packing materials, others use it to cover both supplies and employee time. If you need to add an insurance fee, or other charge based on the total of the order then use global tuning in conjunction with FedEx shipping.

FedEx Account Number

To link FedEx shipping on your Zoovy Merchant web site to your FedEx account, enter the account number here. If you do not have a shipping account with FedEx, you will need to contact them at http://www.fedex.com/. Or call them at the following numbers: U.S. Customer Service: 1-800-463-3339 U.S. International Customer Service: 1-800-247-4747 U.S. TDD Services: 1-800-238-4461

NOTE:

It's worth mentioning that if you don't put in your FedEx number that the shipping system will default to Zoovy's fedex number which quotes high shipping rates, and an origin from zip code 92007.
