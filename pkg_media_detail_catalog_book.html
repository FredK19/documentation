Book Catalog

Overview

The book catalog contains information indexed by ISBN. When uploading to the Book catalog you may use either the ISBN or the Bookland/EAN number. The bookland EAN number is the barcode on the back of the book - and if submitted it will automatically be detected and converted to an ISBN. Searching by EAN is also possible (the EAN will be converted to an ISBN before the search occurs).
