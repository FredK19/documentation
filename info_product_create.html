How to Create a Product

These are simple and straight-forward instructions on how to create a basic product.
This Zoovy doc does not cover every variation or subtlety of product creation. Some customers have special needs for the items that they sell. If you need detailed assistance please schedule an appointment with one of our Zoovy technicians.
How to create a basic product
  • Click on Products in the primary navigation of your Zoovy account.
  • On the following screen that appears, click on Add New Product.
  • Fill out the text boxes to the best of your ability.
  • Click on Save.
