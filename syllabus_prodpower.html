Syllabus: Bulk Product Changes (Optional)

Power Tools Training

You make changes to many products at once by using the Product Power Tool and the Option Power Tool.

Product Power Tool

  • Selecting products in bulk
  • Selecting attributes
  • The Debug button
  • Changes that can be made in bulk
    It is usually a good idea to preview before making changes.

    • Option Power Tool

    The Option Power Tool can be used to assign Manual Store Option Groups to a variety of products at once.
    The Option Power Tool is great for customers with Product Finders on their webstores.
