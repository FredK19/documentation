Issue #159591: eBay International Shipping Countries not appearing

I have international customers claiming my ship calculator does not tell them the rate. Do I have a setting wrong?

Note from customer

Its an issue with my international ebay buyers not being able to see a shipping rate. Which is costing me business daily. was the initial alert..... check any of my ebay auctions. If you change your location to Australia or Ireland etc....cannot see a rate.

Resolution

That calculator is an ebay calculator that they control when you setup your profile to use calculated rate shipping methods. Zoovy doesn't control anything about that calculator.
That being said, you are correct that for some reason ebay's calculator isn't returning a value for some countries. Unfortunately I can't tell you why. You could certainly open a ticket with ebay and try and find out why they cannot return a rate for Australia when using USPS Express Mail International. Perhaps they have some issue using express mail to certain areas.
Other than that, your options are as following:
  1. you could try to add other international shipping types to your profile to see if this issue is only with USPS Express Intl.
  2. You could add the Zoovy Flash Calculator to your listins so that people could use that rate calculator instead. Note that this is going to return the rates that a customer would see when checking out from your store.
  3. You could reconfigure your profile to use either zoovy rates, or a flat ratetype instead of calculated Probably your best bet is #1 and see if an additional shipping type for international will resolve it.

Customer Follow Up

How can I implement the Zoovy Flash Calculator?

Resolution

Installing the flash calculator was not intended to be a Do it yourself task. It's something we normally drop in when we build a custom template.
The documentation is here: Doc 50108
You might look for a more updated/recent auction wizard -- several of those have it built in (it can be enabled by just checking a box)
