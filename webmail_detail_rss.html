Configuring RSS Feeds

General

The ZoovyMail platform is built on top of FuseMail. The folks at Fusemail have put together an excellent tutorial detailing how to integrate RSS feeds into your email.

http://www.fusemail.com/support/docs/rss.pdf

Hints for Zoovy Customers

  • A personalized version of the Zoovy System News is located at http://support.zoovy.com/news.cgi/YOURNAME.rss
    Be sure to replace YOURNAME with your username to get news that relates to your account.
