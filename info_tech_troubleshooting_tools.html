Attaching File to Ticket

Attach a File
  • Step 1. Log into you web manager
  • Step 2. Go to support
  • Step 3. Open up a new support ticket by clicking on Ask Question
  • Step 4. For an existing ticket scroll down to all open ticket and click on
    REVIEW/UPDATE TICKET
  • Step 5. Scroll to the bottom of the ticket and click the Paper Clip Icon
  • Step 6. Browse for your file on your local computer
  • Step 7. Once you have found the file click add attachment
  • Step 8. Once file is attached click done

    METHODS OF CLEARING CACHE AND COOKIES

    This section of the webdoc is intended to help you be able to clear cache and cookies for the different versions of web browsers you may be using
    THE SHIFT AND REFRESH METHOD
  • Step 1. Hold down the [SHIFT] key
  • Step 2. Simultaneously click on the browser refresh button. This will refresh the page you're viewing.
    • CLEAR CACHE AND COOKIES IN INTERNET EXPLORER 6
  • Step 1. Close all web browser windows before taking any steps
  • Step 2. Start>control panel>internet options.
  • Step 3. Under the general tab, look under the temporary internet files section
  • Step 4. Click the Delete Cookies button, click Ok in the Delete Cookies window
  • Step 5. Next click the Delete Files button, make sure the delete all offline content button is checked, click Ok.
  • This process will clear all cached images and cookies allowing you to view the latest update to websites.
    • CLEAR CACHE AND COOKIES IN INTERNET EXPLORER 7
  • Step 1. Close all web browser windows before taking any steps
  • Step 2. Start>control panel>internet options
  • Step 3. Under Browsing History click Delete.
  • Step 4. Under Temporary Internet Files click Delete Files .
  • Step 5. Under Delete Cookies click Delete Cookies.
  • This process will clear all cached images and cookies allowing you to view the latest update to websites.
    • CLEAR CACHE AND COOKIES IN ALL MOZILLA BROWSER VERSIONS 2.0 AND ABOVE
  • Step 1. Under Tools, select options, select the Privacy tab
  • Step 2. Select Clear Now under Private Data
  • Step 3. Make sure Cache and Cookies are selected and click Clear Private Data Now
  • This process will clear all cached images and cookies allowing you to view the latest update to websites.

    • Generating a Systems Info File

    System Info File
  • Step 1. Go to Start
  • Step 2. All Programs
  • Step 3. Accessories
  • Step 4. System Tools
  • Step 5. Click System Info
  • Step 6. Then look for system type
  • Step 7. Highlight System Summary
  • Step 8. Click File
  • Step 9. Select export
  • Step 10. Now you will want to attach the file to a ticket that applies to the problem.
