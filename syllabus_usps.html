Syllabus: USPS Postage Thru Zoovy (Optional)

Endicia Dazzle / USPS Training

You can print your USPS postage through Zoovy Order Manager by integrating it with Endicia Dazzle.

PREREQUISITES

In order to print USPS postage you will need an Endicia Dazzle account. You can obtain this account at www.endicia.com

Overview

  • Tracking and Delivery Confirmation.
  • Automatic emails.
