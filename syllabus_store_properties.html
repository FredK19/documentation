Syllabus: Basic Settings

Basic Zoovy Settings

This is basic training on the essential settings needed to configure your online store to work correctly. These may seem basic but, by addressing them, you are giving your Zoovy business a solid foundation. Click on Setup.
Store Properties Topics
  • Company Information (Under Site Builder)
  • Inventory
  • Shipping
  • Payment Methods
  • Checkout Setup
  • Sales Tax

    • Company Information

    Your Company Information is configured under Setup > Site Builder. Make sure the Company Information on your DEFAULT profile is entered and correct. Without this information, you will not be able to send email to certain customers.
    In the Country field of your Company Information, use "U.S." or "USA" but not "United States."

    Inventory

    Doc 50309
    Click on Setup in the primary navigation, followed by the Inventory link at the top of the Store Properties box. Choose the inventory setting that best meets your needs.

    Shipping

    Doc 50335
    Doc 51156
    Click on Setup and then Shipping in the Store Properties box.
    Special shipping considerations
  • How much do you pay for shipping? Are you covering your costs?
  • How do you currently charge for shipping? If your method suits your needs, there is no need to make changes.
  • Do you have any special exceptions to how you ship? You can establish special exceptions to your shipping, but this will likely require use of the SHIPPING bundle. The SHIPPING bundle is an additional charge to your Zoovy account.

    • Payment Methods

    Doc 50542
    Click on Setup and then Payment Methods in the Store Properties box. Configure and enable the payment methods that you will accept.

    Checkout Setup

    Doc 50305
    Click on Setup and then Checkout Setup in the Store Properties box. As Checkout Setup can be effected by decisions made in Shipping, it is best to do this after Shipping has been configured.

    Sales Tax

    Click on Setup and then Sales Tax in the Store Properties box. Enter the states and/or zip codes for which you will need to charge tax and the appropriate tax rate.

    Homework

    Be certain that you have the following fields configured before going on to the next topic:
  • company information
  • shipping
  • payment methods
  • taxes
  • checkout setup
  • inventory
    These settings represent the base minimum that you need to have a functioning Zoovy account. Please do not try to cut corners. If you need assistance, please schedule a training session at your convenience.
