Issue #109822: Converting Timestamps

I'm trying to play around with our data. (In a duplicate of our database, not the live one.) I need to sort stuff into months, but I don't know how to convert your timestamp and created numbers into a date. I know I can go by the invoice number, but many times an invoice won't actually be paid for in the same month it was originally created.

Resolution

Converting the Epoch time in Microsoft access can be very difficult.
You might find it easier with Enterprise - because MySQL has a "from_unixtime" function which can be run against the database.
