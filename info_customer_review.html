Customer Reviews

Concept Overview

The importance of organic content on your website can not be overstated. Not only is it great for search engines, but recent studies have shown that more and more online buyers are placing a higher emphasis on Product Reviews. In Zoovy, there are several system product layouts that allow you to enable this feature. Alternatively, this feature can also be added to custom product layouts.

On the Product Page

Zoovy has two versions of the reviews piece. One is Ajax/Web 2.0 enabled and one uses a popup. Currently, the ajax enabled reviews is only available in conjunction with the evolution theme. Any of the other themes can use the popup version.
How to Select a Layout that has Reviews
  • Log in to your account at www.zoovy.com
  • Click on the Products tab
  • Click on one of your products in the list on the left
  • Push the 'Edit Web Page'
  • If you are not prompted to select a layout, put the 'layout' link at the top.
  • Select a layout that has the reviews feature (read the description).
  • By Default, Reviews is enabled. If the reviews do not appear, click 'edit' next to the 'product reviews' prompt.
  • Check the Checkbox (this is global for your entire store)
  • Save your changes
  • Click 'edit' next to the reviews icon prompt
  • Select a reviews icon (this is global for your entire store)
  • Save your changes

    • Reviews Manager

    When a review is left for one of your products, it will NOT automatically show up in your store. The review must first be approved.
    Approving New Reviews
  • Log in to your account at www.zoovy.com
  • Click on the Utilities tab
  • Click on the 'Reviews' link
  • Click on the 'UnApproved' link
  • Approve/Edit/Delete the Reviews

    • Adding your own review

    In the product editor, there is a section for 'reviews' where you can add a review.
    You'll notice that there are several more fields here than what is available on the website. This is for forward compatibility. As the popularity of the reviews features grow, we will enable support for the rest of these features.
    You need the CRM bundle inorder to add this feature to your account. You can add the CRM bundle by going to (Setup > Add/Remove Features)here you can add the CRM bundle to your account.
