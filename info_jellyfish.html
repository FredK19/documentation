[[SECTION]To Get Started]
Bing shopping is managed through Microsoft Adcenter. In order to set up Bing Shopping, each merchant must open an account at adcenter.microsoft.com. Payment information is required for the account but no charges will be generated being Bing shopping is free. Merchants will receive an email once the application has been approved. Thereafter, proceed to the steps listed under Configuration & Setup:
[[/SECTION]]

[[SECTION]Configuration & Setup]

Complete this portion in Bing



  • Click on Tools

  • Click on "Bing Shopping Account Management" 

  • Under store settings, enter the store information. 

  • Click save

  • Click on the FTP settings tab

  • Create a username and password. Save this information as it will be added to the Zoovy control panel.

  • Click save

  • Under the categories tab, choose the ones that suit your products and click Save

  • Default settings under Catalog Management are correct: Feed file name is bingshopping. Click save
[[CAUTION]]
It may take up to 72 hours before you see the file listed under catalog management. 
[[/CAUTION]]
[[BREAK]]
Log into the Zoovy control panel:



  • Click on syndication

  • Click on Bing -> Edit and enter the FTP username and password. Bing FTP server is "feeds.adcenter.microsoft.com"

  •  Click save

[[CAUTION]]
For merchants adding products to bing shopping through ftp, a new feed must be uploaded "at least once every 30 days and at most once a day". If this requirement is not met, your products will stop displaying.

[[/CAUTION]]

[[BREAK]]

You will then need to map the categories of products that you want to send to Bing under the Categories tab. Any category that you leave as "-- Not Selected --" will not be submitted to Bing. Merchants will receive another email once the feed has been reviewed and approved. Products may not appear on www.bing.com/shopping for 7-14
business days. 
[[CAUTION]]Only NEW products are allowed on Bing Shopping[[/CAUTION]]
