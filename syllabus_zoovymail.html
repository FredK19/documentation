Syllabus: Zoovymail

Zoovymail Training

This is basic training on how to setup your Zoovymail account. You are encouraged to setup Zoovymail before using a domain with your Zoovy online store.

PREREQUISITES

Before your Zoovymail training, please go to Setup > Domain Hosting > Zoovymail and enable Zoovymail. You will need to add one seat. Your first seat is free of charge.
If you do not have your Company Information filled out completely, it could prevent your Zoovymail account from being provisioned.

Mail Aliases

A Mail Alias is an email address that you will send and receive email. They are constructed in your Zoovymail account under Settings > Mail Aliases.

Mail Rules

Mail Rules can be constructed in Zoovymail to sort, forward or delete mail automatically.
