Why Outsource?

By outsourcing your email you can focus on your core business and rid your company of the expense associated with having your email in house.

Using a business class email service offers features and functionality not available in non-commercial email platforms, in addition to an overall increased level of professionalism, anti-virus and spam protection.

How is my data secure?

FuseMail implements firewalls at all servers within their network to only allow specific data to travel securely to your computer. They also are co-located in world-class Equinix IBX datacenters, known for their extreme physical security.

How does ZoovyMail handle Spam?

Spam is not only a nuisance, but it is also a counter productive entity that can drain the production of your employees and make Email Communications nearly unbearable. At FuseMail we have several techniques in place that virtually put an end to spam some of these techniques include Challenge Response technology and Bayesian spam filtering.

How Does FuseMail handle Viruses?

All messages sent and received via e-mail are scanned and monitored by the FuseScan Virus Engine, in real time, for spam mail and viruses before ever reaching the Email Network preventing any damage to the data contained in your Email Message or our systems. The FuseScan Anti Virus Engine scans various compressed file formats including ZIP, ARJ (JAR), LZH (LHA), RAR, and ACE.

How much diskspace do I get?

The standard diskspace for fusemail accounts is 250mb. If you need more space, open a support ticket and let us know. We can increase this up to 1gb for you at no additional charge, you need only request it.

Additional space, up to 2gb, 3gb, 4gb, requires customer service manager approval. Open a support ticket for these requests also.

At this time, there is no additional charge for more space, however in the future if it becomes necessary that we charge for this service, it will be announced on the recent news and you will be automatically billed.

Smaller mailboxes load faster and have less problems with caching/corruption.
