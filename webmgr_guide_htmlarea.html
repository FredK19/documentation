HTML Editor Guide

Special keys:

CTRL-A -- select all
CTRL-B -- bold
CTRL-I -- italic
CTRL-U -- underline
CTRL-S -- strikethrough
CTRL-L -- justify left
CTRL-E -- justify center
CTRL-R -- justify right
CTRL-J -- justify full
CTRL-1 .. CTRL-6 -- headings (>h1< .. >h6<)
CTRL-0 (zero) -- clean content pasted from Word
