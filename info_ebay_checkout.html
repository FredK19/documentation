eBay Only Checkout

Concept Overview

This document is to help you convert from eBay checkout redirect to the new eBay only Checkout.
Zoovy can now simplify your ebay checkout experience. Converting to eBay checkout is virtually seamless. When we switch you over to eBay checkout, it accomplishes the following 3 things:
  • 1. switches token setting (In Zoovy Profile)
  • 2. attempts to revise all live ebay listings (In eBay)
  • 3. sets global "EBAY-CHECKOUT" flag (In Zoovy Profile)
    • Be aware that Zoovy can only update your listings that have the ability to be edited per eBay guideline

    Getting Started

    The most important thing you must do to prepare for eBay checkout is to add a merchant account to eBay if you want the ability to accept Credit Card payments for your eBay orders. This is only mandatory if you use a processor other then paypal for credit card orders (i.e Authorize.net) Zoovy eBay Checkout Redirect will be discontinued on 6/10/09 and we will automatically convert your checkout method. However if you would like to be proactive and make the switch sooner, please setup your credit card merchant account with eBay and then open up a ticket with support to do the rest.
    If you accept credit cards for ebay orders you must setup a FREE "Paypal Payflow Pro" gateway in your ebay account, which is compatible with most (but not all) merchant accounts.

    Configure Merchant Account on Ebay

  • 1. Go to your "My eBay"
  • 2. Account
  • 3. Select "Site Preferences"
  • 4. Go to "Payment from buyers" and click edit
  • 5. For security reasons you may or may not be prompted to login again
  • 6. You will then be redirected to "Payment Preferences"
  • 7. Go to "Merchant Credit Card Preferences"
  • 8. Click on "Sign up for Payflow payment gateway"
  • 9. Click the button labeled "Register with Payflow"
  • 10. Next you will fill in the form and all the required fields.
  • ** Please note that you may not know all the requested information in this form. If this is the case, you must contact your bank/merchant account provider/financial firm, and ask them for the requested info, and/or assistance in filling out the form.
    • Zoovy support regrettably, cannot help you fill out the Payflow Pro Signup (we don't have that information)
