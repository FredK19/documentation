Browser Type Support

Overview

Currently Zoovy is focused on supporting the latest versions of Internet Explorer (IE) and Mozilla Firefox. This includes the latest full releases of each. Beta or previous versions that are no longer supported by their own productive companies are not supported by Zoovy.
This document refers to the Zoovy.com management user interface, NOT the websites produced by it. The compatibility of a website is determined by it's own HTML code and specific functionality (e.g. AJAX) - and can vary from site to site.

Other Browsers

Browsers including Chrome, Opera, Safari, and others that may be unlisted are not thoroughly tested or supported by our technical staff. Select issues regarding these browsers may be considered for support through our technical and development staff depending on the severity of the issue seen.

AOL Support

Support for AOL becomes very difficult in many cases because they set their own rules and conventions. Many times these rules are different than those set by the largest market share browsers (IE and Firefox). In order to provide you with the best features for your web presence, we try to adapt the newest stable trends internet/browser functionality. Unfortunately, this can mean at times that the display for AOL users is less than optimal.
