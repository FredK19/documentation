Wholesale Feature Set Training (Optional)

With Zoovy you can assign special pricing and privileges to a group of specific customers or for a specific marketplace or specialty site.
This feature requires the addition of the Wholesale Bundle which may be an additional charge.

Overview

  • Configure pricing schedule.
  • Wholesale price per product.
  • Assigning a customer to a wholesale schedule.
  • Quantity pricing.
    You must have the Wholesale Bundle to let other retailers sell your products via the JEDI suply chain feature.

    • Homework

    Test any wholesale schedule that you build.
