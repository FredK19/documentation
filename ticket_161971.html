Issue #161971: Orders not receiving free shipping promotion

We had an order placed and the customer did not receive the "Free Shipping" promotion. We have have a free shipping promo going on and this is the first that had a problem.

Steps to troubleshoot

  • Looked at order.
  • Created same order in cart.
  • Put in Zip code
  • Saw free shipping method (default), and another option "Shipping $10.99" was also available.

    • Resolution

    Right off the bat - there are two ways I think this could have happened:
    1. your shipping promotion does not disable the 10.99 shipping - so the customer can still select it.
    2. if the customer had selected shipping PRIOR to the promotion, and came back and proceeded through checkout (or re-ordered from a previous order that did not have free shipping) then it could also happen.
    Remember: If you don't take away the choice (even if it's not the best choice) - it can still be selected.
