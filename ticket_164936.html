Issue

Issue: Supply Chain sends zoovy product id, instead of supplier sku.
Automated supply chain order emails show the Product ID in the SKU column instead of the actual MFG SKU - Supplier gets confused and isn't sure which product to ship out. This is affecting my business - orders get delayed.

Resolution

Try setting the field zoovy:prod_supplierid instead of zoovy:prod_mfgid
