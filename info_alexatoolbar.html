Alexa Toolbar Webpage Information

Overview

Alexa (www.alexa.com) has a toolbar that some merchants find useful. This toolbar is not direct supported by Zoovy, not is Alexa a current partner. As such, any issues with their functionality falls under 3rd party integration and BPP policy.Doc 50850

Info Button

One of the main features of the Alexa toolbar is their "Info" button. This will display information such as contact name, address, phone number, email address, and site statistics for the web page currently being displayed.
This information is pulled from InterNIC by Alexa. The problem is that usually your information will display with Zoovy's contact information, rather than your own. You can read more about it here:
http://www.alexa.com/site/help/?index=22
To address the information that Alexa displays in that information area (as instructed in the url above), you can go to the following url and either disable it or change the informaiton:
http://www.alexa.com/data/details/editor?type=contact
