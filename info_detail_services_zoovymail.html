Zoovy Mail Hosting / Transport service

Zoovy Mail Hosting

Zoovy does not provide its customers with email accounts by default. We feel this is the responsibility of the customers Internet Service Provider. Zoovy does provide domain redirection services which provide email forwarding if you own a customer domain name. In addition, you may purchase a Zoovymail account to add your existing Zoovy bundle.

Zoovymail Account

Zoovy offers mail accounts as an added feature to a Zoovy account. The standard package is 5 emails accounts linked to a domain hosted by Zoovy. Each mail box having 250 megs of storage space for the mail.

Autoresponders

Autoresponders can be configured with an email or hosting account. You must provide the mailbox name e.g. info@yourdomain.com, along with the message.
The autoresponder responds to all inbound messages, except those from other auto-responders. Up to 5 addresses with different auto-responders are available. With ZoovyMail service, you can have unlimited emails - up to 20mb of disk space.

Mail Filtering

By default Zoovy uses Spamcop and ORDB to filter our mail. If you are using a Zoovymail account, there is additional filtering taking place.

Here are the filters currently, on ZoovyMail accounts.

scr, pif, exe, 386, app, bin, btm, com, cab, cbt, cgi, chm, cmd, crt, cvp, dll, dot, drv, fon, fxp, hlp, isp, jar, jse, ksh, lib, lnk, mp3, nte, nws, obj, ocx, ops, pgm, pot, pps, prg, reg, slb, smm, sys, url, vir, vbs, vmx, vxd, xms

These are the extensions that are blocked to and from ZoovyMail, they are NOT account customizable, this is for every account. If a zip file comes in with a name like this it is also blocked:

your_document|approved|thank_you|details|wicked|your_details|application|document|screensaver|movie)\.zip

Blank Subject lines are blocked as SPAM. Improperly created TO lines in mail will be blocked. Spammers try to hide their mail routes and return addresses.

If mail is blocked for any of these reasons, ZoovyMail will bounce the message with a very specific error message as to why it bounced.
