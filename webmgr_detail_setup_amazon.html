Amazon Merchant Feed

Overview

Congratulations from Amazon Services! Once you've successfully created an Amazon Marketplace account, then you're ready to start selling at Amazon.com.
Access Seller Central, the Amazon site-management system, at the following URL: http://sellercentral.amazon.com
As a seller, you can use Seller Central to:
  • set your own shipping rates
  • get your Merchant Token
  • get your Merchant Name
  • provide gift messaging and gift-wrap services
  • view metrics to help you manage your business

    Amazon is very liberal with their suspensions!!
    Any bad feedback, may endanger your account status with them. Be sure to respond to any/all negative feedback in a timely manner. Zoovy's policy is such that we cannot assist in any reinstatements of Amazon suspensions. To ensure your success, we recommend you take advantage of our Amazon training.

    If you have any questions as you begin listing products and setting up your seller account, please visit Amazon Seller Central Help Center:
    http://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/help-home.html
    How do I get an Amazon Token?
    If you have contacted Amazon about setting up an account and haven't received a response, please contact your Zoovy Account Rep. They have been known to get you a Token fairly quickly.
