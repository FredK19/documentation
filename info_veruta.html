Veruta Remarketing Integration

Concept Overview

Veruta is now a supported Zoovy Partner. Veruta is an industry leader in online advertising. Their proprietary technology and solution democratizes the online banner advertising arena and provides a simple to use tool that allows advertisers to customize their own banners and select which products to display. By utilizing the right set of tools, merchants are able to utilize banner advertisements to not only drive more traffic, but also build their brand and effectively merchandise products that ultimately lead to repeat customers and incremental revenue.
Veruta isn't just about retargeting. It's about giving you the ability to continue to communicate with your customers after they've left your site. It's about letting you showcase your products and promotions. And it's about putting you in control of your budget.

    • How does it work / How much does it cost

    How much does it cost?

    Zoovy has a special relationship with Veruta. Our clients do not pay normal CPE (Cost Per Experience fees) which can be very expensive and difficult to measure. Instead Zoovy clients pay a flat $5.50 base CPM (Cost Per Thousand Impressions) for branding/exposure. Zoovy clients also gain access to other exclusive veruta features which are normally reserved for large agencies with substantial budgets. Zoovy clients receive the same transaction rates as very large agencies, however unlike large agencies our clients do not pay a minimum fee. You will only pay for what you use, and a nominal rev. share to Veruta for the resulting sales your site generates. Veruta charges will appear on your normal Zoovy invoice.
    Zoovy does not set a daily or monthly budget, defaults to $999.99 daily for each ad. It is important for merchants to set a daily budget for each campaign/banner. Decide on monthly budget divide by 30 days, then divide by 3 (or the number of active banners) By not setting a budget, the charges will be based on your traffic and visitor tagging.

    Where will my Banners appear?

    Simply put: Veruta advertisements appear on sites your customers visit when they navigate away from your site (to sites that display advertisements). Veruta will only show advertisements to shoppers who have been tagged, and you only pay for advertising they deliver to your clients. Veruta currently reaches about 80% of the content sites on the Internet through its solid relationships with major media networks but the demographics vary based on your shoppers.

    Configuring Veruta

    Before proceeding, please click the 'Learn More' link in the Syndication >> Veruta interface for information regarding common questions about Veruta configuration. Further help can be found by emailing questions@veruta.com or calling (408) 577-1811. Once you've familiarized yourself with the Veruta product, click Next: Let's Get Started!

    Account Creation


  • Go to Syndication >> Veruta: Select a profile, and click the Setup Account link (this path leads to the same page as using the Next: Let's Get Started! button referenced in the hint above.
  • Choose a campaign type, by checking the appropriate box(es). At least one campaign type must be selected for the configuration to be valid. For help deciding which type of campaign to use, consult the 'Learn More' in Zoovy syndication.

    Campaign Types
    -> Static Banner w/ Coupon + phone number (does not pull in products - static brand banner)
    -> Dynamic Product Banner w/ Individual product recommendations. (will send all products)
    -> Dynamic New Products List (products must be tagged as new)
    -> Dynamic Best Seller List (products must be tagged as best sellers)
    -> Dynamic On Sale Now List (products must be tagged as on sale)
    -> Flash Deal of the Day* (Flash Deal of Day will require development to create or to customize an existing flash and therefore cannot be done as a fixed price express project.)
    -> Other / Custom (also not done as a fixed price express project)
    More on Product Tagging

    Once products are tagged, they will be the only products displayed in the Veruta banners. If you want all products to be sent select the Dynamic Product Banner w/Individual product recommendation.
    Merchants choosing specific dynamic lists must tag products before the Veruta ads will run. If no products are tagged for the ad, then products can not be pulled in.
    Select which 'Scope' option you would like our design department to use in creating your Veruta banner(s).
    Express Artwork recommended, however there is no customization or approval for this scope
    Now you're ready to create your Veruta account. Clicking the Create Account button will create a Veruta account, and open a corresponding support ticket for the Zoovy design department. Post any special instructions regarding your artwork into this ticket, which can be accessed in the Support section of your Zoovy account.
    Once your banners (also known as creatives) have been completed, they will automatically be loaded into your Veruta account. When Veruta acknowledges the creatives, the merchant will be contacted via email.

    • Website Visitor Tagging

    Settings control which, and how often visitors are tagged for retargeting. Zoovy campaigns do not have daily budgets.
    Daily budget needs to be set up in Veruta to control monthly spend. Especially encouraged for merchants with high traffic sites.
    Merchants should use below settings to only re-target appropriate traffic
    Tagging helps control budget and improve conversions

  • Tagging Probability - 1:1 ALL Vistors Tagged; 1:2 1 out of every 2 tagged, etc
  • Tagging Events - qualifying events for tagging, select 1 or all
    Options: Products Viewed, Categories Viewed, Cart Page, Checkout.
  • Minimum Qualifying Events - how many events/actions there need to be before shopper is tag
    When setting veruta up, please keep in mind the following information regarding 'Qualifying Events':
    Here is an explanation how to read the example output:

    recorded_events = tracks the total number of qualifying events the user has performed.
    required_events = is how many qualifying events the user must perform before the veruta code is displayed.
    So what is a "Qualifying Event" well based on the example above:

    tag_products=0 means a product view is NOT a qualifying event
    tag_category=0 means a category view is NOT a qualifying event
    tag_cart=1 means a cart view/add to cart IS a qualifying event
    tag_checkout=1 means entering checkout IS a qualifying event


    • Disabling Veruta

    To stop the Veruta campaign do the following: In Zoovy under Syndication Â» Veruta Remarketing
  • Click Edit for the active profile
  • Under account setting login to veruta
  • Check all banners (defaults to running banners page)
  • Under Status you will see Resume Pause, click Pause
    To make sure you have paused them, in the Status Running | Paused | All, click Running. If paused no banners names should be listed, once verified you may close the veruta window
  • Go back to the Zoovy window, under Website Visitor Tagging >> Tagging Probability, change to 0% disabled
  • Lastly Save at the bottom
    Veruta is now disabled, however you will receive final billing for the program. Billing can be seen under the billing tab in Veruta syndication.
