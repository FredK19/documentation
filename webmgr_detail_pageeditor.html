Site Builder - Page Editor

Overview

You can get to the page editor by going to Setup / Website Builder - from there you will be able to edit your about us, shopping cart, gallery, return policy, privacy policy, and any other page you want to (including your category pages). The only pages on your site that you cannot edit directly from the website builder is the product pages, which are edited directly from the product itself in the online product editor.

Product Layout Properties - Detailed Description

Detailed descriptions are descriptions that are NOT the same as your product description. Some of the standard page layouts include a detailed description, which can be different than your product description. This is especially useful if you want to have one thing which appears in your auctions, and another which appears on your site. Or if you want one thing to appear in your product list (e.g. a non-HTML version of the product list) and another which appears on the actual product page.

Configuring a Product List

You can configure your products to appear as thumbnails by selecting a thumbnail product list in the website builder. To configure simply go to Setup | Website Builder | click the edit link for the page you want to have thumbnails. Finally select [Edit] link next to the product list, then change the layout to thumbnail and select how many columns you want.

Layouts with Multiple Product Lists and/or specialty store homepages

Some layouts support multiple product lists. To utilize this feature, you must first create 'lists' in the categories and lists interface, then select them in the site builder.
Creating and selecting lists
  • Log in to your account at www.zoovy.com
  • Click on the setup tab.
  • Click on the 'Categories & Lists' link
  • Push the 'add new' button and enter a list name. Be sure to select the 'product list' option.
  • Save the list
  • Create more lists if needed)
  • Scroll down the page and click the arrow to the left of your list.
  • Push the 'add product' button and select products for the list. Save your changes.
  • Save your changes.
  • Add products to each of the lists you created if multiple lists were created.
  • Click the setup tab
  • Click the site builder link
  • Click 'edit' next to the page you want to modify
  • Click 'edit' next to one of the product lists
  • When the editor appears, set the 'Load Products from' option to one of the lists.
  • Save your changes
  • Select a separate list for each of the extra product lists on the page.
    • You may be asking yourself "Self, why don't they remove the 'products' link from the other prodlist elements?" The answer is because this method gives you the opportunity to have prodlist 1 reference a 'list' and prodlist 3 reference the default homepage products. It's more versatile this way. Especially since there are other system implications for how the products on the homepage are used (ex: related items).

    Page Properties - Title and Description

    At the top of the page editor in the website builder, there is a button for page properties. In this area, you can modify the page title, description and keywords, so as to optimize your site for search engine registration. Changing the description and keywords area will not effect how this page appears to your customers. However, the page title is displayed in most themes/wrappers to the customer, plus it is used at the top of the browser itself.
    'Change Page Title and Meta Information'
  • Login to your account at http://www.zoovy.com
  • Click on the Setup tab.
  • Click the Website Builder link.
  • Click on the edit button next to the category page you would like to modify.
  • Click on the Page Properties button at the top of the page.
  • Make you changes in the appropriate text boxes.
  • Save your changes.

    • When using the "Live Preview" functionality to preview your edits to that page layout, you may encounter that the browser's "Back" button simply reloads the preview. To get back you will need to double click the "Back" button to get you back to the page layout. Alternately, if you right click on the "Live Preview" link, you can either open the preview in another window, or in another tab window, which will allow you to modify the layout and still see your results. (You will just need to "Refresh" the preview window between modifications.)
