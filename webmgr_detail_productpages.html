Configuring Product Pages

Designing Your Product Page

Each time you create a new product, the default Zoovy page layout (page flow as we call them here) is selected. At any time, you can change this to better accomodate this particular products needs. Wide, Long, and multiple picture formats are available. Can't find a flow that meets your needs? Talk to your salesperson about having a custom flow designed. Currently, there is no way to specify your default product page layout.
'Changing Your Product Page Layout (Page Flow)'
  • Login to your account at http://www.zoovy.com
  • Click on the Products tab.
  • Click on the product you would like to modify.
  • Scroll down and click on the 'Design Web Page' button.
  • From this list, choose the appropriate flow by clicking on the thumbnail.
  • Your change will automatically be saved.

    • Showing Multiple Pictures

    Many layouts feature more than one picture. You will want to find a layout which showcases your products appropriately.
