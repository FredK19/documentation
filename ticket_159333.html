Due to the fact that you are doing a 301 redirect on your koie.zoovy.com domain which is permanent. Your mail will no longer function for that domain. This is due to the fact of when your redirecting a domain you are telling everyone this domain is no longer valid. Therefore your domain will not be able to receive mail. Solution #2

The sub-user on the zoovy side doesn't have email enabled.

This problem seems pretty obvious (unless I'm missing something) - the user you are trying to access is not a valid alias for admin@username.zoovy.com and does not have a separate mailbox.
There are two ways this can be corrected:
  1. You can create an alias for user@yourdomain.com in the admin@username.zoovy.com -- OR --
  2. You can login to the zoovy site as the administrator, go to setup / user logins delete the user, and then re-add this account with email. This will create a separate email mailbox for subuser@username.zoovy.com along with aliases for all your domains.
