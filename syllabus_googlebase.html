Syllabus: GoogleBase

GoogleBase Training

GoogleBase is a free service that submits your product data to Google and to Google Product Search (formerly known as Froogle).
By using GoogleBase syndication you will also have your products automatically submitted to Google Product Search.

GoogleBase Overview

  • Go to base.google.com
  • Register the file "products.xml"
  • Create an ftp account
  • Save your ftp account information into Zoovy
    Any product that appears on your webstore will be submitted to GoogleBase.

    • Specialty Sites

    You can submit a feed file to Google Base for each of your specialty sites by registering a new bulk upload file for each profile.
    We advise you to have one GoogleBase account for every profile in Zoovy. The best practice for least confusion is to create a new Google account based on the email aliases created for that profile's domain in your Zoovymail account.

    Homework

    Enter your GoogleBase account information under Syndication > GoogleBase and save. The top of the screen should say "Pending - Next 24 hours."
