Issue #159405: Need to merge two customer accounts into one

Issue

Issue: when changing customer email - received ERROR: The email address user@domain.com is already in use by customer #
Customer requested please change my username from my old email address: user@olddomain.com to my current email address: user@newdomain.com

When I used order manager to find customer by email, I did. Then I pressed edit, but was unable to edit the customer email because the new email address already existed. What do I do?

Resolution

Merge two customer accounts
  • Lookup user@newdomain.com
  • Write down the order #'s
  • Delete the user@newdomain.com
  • Change the email address from user@olddomain.com to user@newdomain.com
  • Reassociate any orders that were written down to the new account
    • I have written this down as a feature request (the ability to merge customer accounts).
