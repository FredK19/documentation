Payment Gateways

Overview

This document covers the basic operation of a gateway, along with which gateways are supported and their various subtle differences.

Why do I need a Payment Gateway?

Payment gateways authorize credit card transactions over the internet in real time. The card number is input through the checkout system and goes immediately to the gateway for authorization. Your customer is then immediately charged, or the card is denied. The funds are processed each night and deposited to your account. The time it takes to have the funds in your account depends on your payment gateway and merchant account.

Linkpoint

Linkpoint gateway is a service by Cardservice that allow you to conduct real-time credit card and automatic check processing services over the Internet. Zoovy supports the linkpoint API from Cardservice, in addition to several other gateways which are compatible with other processors. For more information about the Linkpoint service please visit: Doc 50280

Verisign Payflow Pro

Zoovy supports Verisign Payflow Pro.

Username, password, reseller

In order to learn your username, password, and reseller information you will need to contact Verisign technical support services.
Make sure you verify your "Vendor name" with Verisign technical support or you will not be able to process transactions.
