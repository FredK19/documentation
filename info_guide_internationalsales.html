Selling Internationally

International Auctions

What is the first thing that comes to mind when you think of auctioning your items internationally? You may be worried about the money conversion rates, shipping, payment, or communicating with the bidders. The positive thing about auctioning internationally is that you have the opportunity to bring even more people to your Zoovy store. Once your Zoovy store has been setup to accept international customers, you can tap into a brand new market at minimal cost.

Payment

If you decide to accept international orders, clearly indicate payments in the form of money orders, cash, or checks must be in U.S. dollars or your country's standard currency. If you fail to state this, you may have to convert the money into another form of currency and pay the associated fees. Credit cards like Visa and Master Card provide the easiest means of accepting international payments. They will convert the customer's currency to your own at no cost to you. You can accept wire transfers to have the money directly deposited into your account. However, the fee for this service is quite costly.

International Tax

When shipping a product to another country, it may be subject to additional taxes. For example, certain parts of Europe are subject to VAT or Value Added Tax, which may be assessed when a package reaches customs. Legibly complete all of the necessary forms to ensure that packages travel through customs as smoothly as possible. Specifically, you will need forms 2976-A and 2976-E for most international shipments.
Regardless of what the customer requests - you should never accommodate a winning bidder by marking a package, "gift." This is considered mail and customs fraud and carries a very stiff penalty.

International Shipping

Clearly indicate to bidders that international shipping is more costly than domestic shipping. If you have already configured your Zoovy store to accept international orders, the winning bidder simply goes to your web site and chooses the desired form of shipping. The process is streamlined and the rates are automatically calculated for the customers.

High Risk International Countries

It is up to you to determine your acceptable level of risk. There are certain international countries that are designated as high risk countries because of civic instability, or high incidents of credit card fraud.
Zoovy periodically updates its list of high risk countries. However in general they include countries that the US Postal service will not insure packages to.
You can still ship to high risk countries, however it is recommend you only accept checks and money orders and always ensure you have been paid before shipping out a product.

Foreign currencies

Zoovy does not officially support foreign language sites. Some customers have found it is possible to completely change all messaging throughout Zoovy to foreign languages. Zoovy is not equipped to support non-US currencies, or to support non-US origin shipping. The shipping can be overcome by using the Shipping API. Virtually all messaging in the system can be edited by simply creating a custom site wrapper, custom site buttons, and changing all checkout messages (with requies the WEB bundle).

Troubleshooting

In order to allow customers from International destinations to checkout from your Zoovy store you need both a shipping method and a payment method enabled for International destinations. Make sure if you want to ship to both low and high risk countries to enable the "Anywhere In the World" setting in the Setup->Shipping configuration as well as a payment method for "United States and Low and High Risk Countries".
