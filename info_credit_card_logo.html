Add Credit Card Logo to Store

Credit Card Logos

This web doc will provide the steps necessary to add credit card logos and other logos to your website [TAG]discover=1000]
If you have a custom theme please ask your designer for assistance with adding credit card logos to your webstore.

How to enable logos

Click by Click Steps
  • Step 1 Click on setup
  • Step 2 Click on site builder
  • Step 3 Under site theme click on the sidebar link
  • Step 4 Select the logo from the available logo list
  • Step 5 Use the right arrow to move the selected logo to the list of selected logos

    • You can only select one logo at a time. Meaning you can't select multiple logos at once and move them from the available to selected logos list in one move. You will have to move them one at a time. In addition you can use the move up or move down button to the right to determine the order of how the logos will appear. In addition, if you would like to remove a selected logo simply select the logo under the list of selected logos and use the left arrow. It will move the selected logo back under the available logo section. Always remember to click SAVE after completing your changes or modifications.
