Syllabus: Domain Hosting

Domain Hosting Training

This is basic training for transferring or forwarding a domain name to your Zoovy online store.

PREREQUISITES

Before you go into this training, please ensure that you have completely and correctly filled out your Company Information found under Setup > Site Builder.

Domain Hosting Survival Guide

What are you trying to do?
  • I am registering and purchasing a brand new domain through Zoovy. Go to the Doc 50857
  • I am transferring my domain from another host (such as GoDaddy, Network Solutions, etc.) to Zoovy. Go to the Doc 50858
  • I am pointing a domain from another host (such as GoDaddy, Network Solutions, etc.) to Zoovy. Go to the Doc 50859

    • Domain Hosting

    Adding a domain name to your online store also transfers the e-mail associated to that domain name.

    Add Domains

    Use the "Add Domain" button in order to register, transfer or link a domain.

    When to use which button:

    REGISTER: Use this option if you have an idea for a domain you would like to register via Zoovy. You will be charged $10 per year for this domain once it has been registered for you. Registration is usually complete in a couple of hours. See more information at Doc 50857.

    TRANSFER: Use this option if you already own a domain, and would like to transfer it to Zoovy. Be sure to see the document on transferring domains prior to getting started - Doc 50859.

    LINK: Use this option if you have a domain registered elsewhere (GoDaddy, Network Solutions, etc) and want to keep it there, but point the domain to your Zoovy store. Be sure to see the section on Doc 50859.

    Domain Config

    In Domain Config, you can set preferences for the profile that will be used and where the e-mail will go.

    Zoovy Mail

    This is where you retrieve your Zoovy Mail password. Zoovy Mail aliases can be built in webmail.zoovy.com based on the domains that you have entered in Domain Hosting.
    You must change your nameservers to ns.zoovy.com and ns2.zoovy.com before you transfer your domain to Zoovy.
