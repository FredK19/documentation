Improving Sales

Overview

Zoovy can help to improve your sales in several ways. First Zoovy gives you the tools create a professional appearance throughout a customers entire e-commerce experience. Second Zoovy incorporates tools such as auction integration and search engine registration to allow you to attract new business. Third Zoovy also gives you the tools you need to generate repeat business by having excellent customer service tools combined with state of art customer tracking and reporting!

What is the problem?

Probably your first step should be to analyze and figure out what your problem is. Our integration with Google Analytics makes it easy to determine if you are having trouble getting traffic to your site or if they are getting to your site and dropping off before making a purchase. These are two distinctly different situations that need to be approached differently.

Do you have products available for purchase?

This may seem like an obvious question but you would be surprised how often this is the reason for low sales volume.
In order to make sales you must have products with prices, inventory, and pictures available on your website and/or other marketplaces.
Do you have products available for purchase?
  • Do you have products under the Products tab of your Zoovy account?
  • Do these products each have the Sale Price filled in? You cannot sell anything without a price.
  • Do your product have inventory? Check the On Shelf Quantity found in the Inventory section of the product. Is there a positive number here?
  • Is the product on your web store? Check the Website Navigation section of your products. How many categories has it been mapped to?

    • Syndication

    Syndication lets you broadcast your products to marketplaces where buyers go to shop for products. To configure syndication for any marketplace simply login to Zoovy.com and go to the syndication tab.

    GoogleBase

    GoogleBase is easy to setup, does not reserve inventory and it is free. If you have the XSELL flag (i.e., the Cross-sell Bundle) it is a no-brainer. You should be using GoogleBase.

    Amazon

    Amazon is a strong marketplace but not everyone can sell there. Amazon is "invite only." Further, they have many different kinds of accounts. You want to make certain that you get a Seller Central account. If you are interested in applying, you may do so at sellercentral.amazon.com

    EBay

    Even sellers who feel they cannot make money off of EBay may find some use from this monolithic marketplace as a customer acquisition tool. Although other marketplaces have made significant headway, EBay remains a popular site for first time e-commerce shoppers. With Zoovy, your EBay auctions close to your Zoovy site where the customer is given a chance to add to his/her order and subscribe to your newsletter. As a Zoovy seller, use EBay to find customers who are interested in the product you sell. Next time, rather than go to EBay they may go directly to your webstore.

    Newsletter

    Using the Newsletter included in the CRM bundle can provide a quick boost for lagging sales.
