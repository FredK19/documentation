Introduction to Zoovy Order Manager

Overview

The Zoovy Order Manager brings the power of your Zoovy website right to your desktop. You can immunize your business from intermitent Internet access, and slow downloads. You can also provide employees with a restricted environment to perform their work, and use this tool to send targeted emails, and print invoices. You can even use Order Manager to set up a shipping station and have invoices, packing slips and shipping labels printed right where you do your shipping.
Zoovy Order Manager is part of Zoovy Integrated Desktop.

Inventory Integration

Order Manager automatically synchronizes inventory with your store - even while you are working offline. Order Manager records changes to your inventory levels and uploads the revised data the next time you synchronize and update data.

Offline Point Of Sale - POS Functionality

Order Manager can create invoices for customers, even if you're offline and then synchronize those orders when your Internet connection is re-established.

Order Split/Combine

Allows the user to easily split invoices for orders with backordered items, or to combine multiple orders from the same customer.

Query Shipping from your Store

As an order is built and changed; the customer is presented with updated shipping costs using rates that would be quoted on the store.

Prints Packing Slips / Invoices

Order Manager prints professional invoices, and packing slips with your company logo, address, and other guides. Invoices and Packing slips also include a barcode which can be used with most shipping systems.
Some Zoovy merchants include a return/RMA print out on the back of their invoices/packing slips. Effectively they just run the paper through the printer twice.

Prints Shipping Labels

Order Manager supports Dymo, and Seiko thermal label printers. In addition, Order Manager can print on Avery labels via inkjet or LaserJet printers and automatically tracks where the last sheet ended so you never throw away half used sheets of labels.

HTML Email Support

Order Manager can send HTML based email to customers, along with an embedded plaintext copy so your customers can always read your emails. Supports an unlimited number of custom templates. Order Manager even automatically creates unsubscribe URLs so that customers who do not want to receive future newsletters can update their account profiles on your website and opt-out of future mailings.

Customer Search

Order manager builds a fast lookup table so you can easily search for customers. It will also let you edit customer records, and view a customer's order history.

Reporting

Order Manager includes over a dozen built in reports to help you manage your business, including sales, disposition, and commission tracking reports.

Customizable Email Templates

Order manager allows you to add your own customized email templates; this can be automatically customized with information from the customer records such as the customer's first name, last name, or other guides. Sending personalized email is a great way to help customers remember you.

Prints Airbills/Imports Tracking

Order Manager can create airbills for FedEx, and UPS, as well as print postage using the Endicia Dazzle software. Order manager already has built in filters to import tracking numbers from most popular shipping applications. Those tracking numbers are automatically associated with the order, and can be accessed by customers via their account history on the website (or can be automatically emailed to your customers). Most Zoovy clients prefer to use Order Manager to send their tracking notification emails, because Order Manager can send customized HTML Emails which look much more professional than the ones created by their shipping carrier's application.

Payment Processing

Order Manager can automatically settle credit cards which were authorized on the website using your payment gateway. Allow your customers a choice of paypal, checks, money orders, etc.

Third Party Integration

Order Manager supports integration with a variety of 3rd party applications (see the 3rd party section for a full list of compatible applications). Most popular small business applications are supported including QuickBooks, Endicia.

Export Customer Relations Management - CRM Information

Order manager includes the ability to export or email lists of customers. Includes over 25 built in reports to sample customers based on their purchase frequency, history, amounts purchased, types of products purchased, or even geographic location.

Auto-Email Capabilities

Order Manager can automatically email customers on events such as orders changing status, tracking numbers being processed, or any number of other events.
