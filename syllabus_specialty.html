Specialty Site Hosting Training

This is basic training for specialty site hosting.
You want to master one Zoovy store before you start running multiple Zoovy stores. For that reason, specialty site hosting is reserved for late in your training.

PREREQUISITES

You must have at least one domain in order to use specialty site hosting.
