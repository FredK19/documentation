Training Syllabus: Checkout Setup

This document will give you some tips on training for the Checkout Setup screen. There are many choices to be made by the customer. Most of these choices will be self-evident by reading the screen but some of the more esoteric concepts are explained below.
Customers with the WEB flag have significantly more options on the Checkout Setup screen than those without the WEB flag. If the customer does not have the WEB flag, the only option available here will be Website Name.

Enable Adult Warning

Currently it is not possible only have certain pages on your site protected by the Adult warning. It is either fully protected or not protected at all.

ICQ Number

ICQ is a type of instant messenger. If the customer has an ICQ number, he or she may enter it. If not, it is not something that will be needed in order to move forward.

Display Shipping Total in Cart

This choice can affect the calculation of shipping. It can also streamline the checkout process for a customer. Most customers are going to quote some kind of shipping in the cart. If he or she is not going to ever quote shipping, the first choice is applicable. If the customer uses a Fixed Rate, Simple Shipping, Weight-Based Shipping, or Price-Based Shipping, he or she can safely quote without Zip Code. In this case, the location of the customer is irrelevant. If our customer is quoting Fed Ex, UPS, DHL or USPS, the location that is being shipped to is pivotal. In this case, the customer will want to require a Zip Code in order to get his or her shipping quote.
The method in which a customer calculates shipping is relevant to when they should quote shipping. A rate coming from UPS or FedEx, for example, would require a zip code.

Account Creation

The point at which an account is created effects the kind of customer service the Zoovy store owner will offer. By having some kind of account creation, the Zoovy store owner is allowing his or her customers to log into their accounts and retrieve information such as order status and tracking information.

Blank Price Message

This message is displayed when the Base Price field (or Sell For Price) is left blank. It is useful for something the customer wants to display as a product on his or her website but does not want to sell.

Checkout Phone Number Settings

Checkout Phone Number Settings are located below the Save and Exit buttons and easy to overlook.
