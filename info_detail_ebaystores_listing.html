About this Tool

The eBay Stores channel is designed to add a single item into a sellers eBay Store. In order to use this Channel the seller must have signed up for an eBay store which is an additional service from eBay.

Operational Details

This channel will typically go from pending to active within 30 minutes of being created. Once it has been picked up by the eBay system it will be added immediately.

Revoking, Removing, or otherwise deleting this channel will cause Zoovy to remove the item.

When an item is purchased, an incomplete item is created and an email is automatically sent to the customer.

Special Notes

This channel is NOT compatible with eBay motors.
Per eBay Rules - You must have a rating (feedback) of 10 or higher to use the "Buy It Now" feature.
Per eBay Rules - You must have a rating (feedback) of 50 or higher to dutch (multiple) item sales.


Store Duration and GTC (Good Till Canceled)

Merchants intending to utilize this feature must take precautions in their ebay.com account to ensure listings are *NOT* upgraded from 30 day listings to Good Till Canceled (GTC) listings. Failure to take precautions may result in loss of privileges of this feature.
Common ways this situation occurs:
  • 1. eBay sends emails following the end of a 30 day listing offering to upgrade it to GTC.
  • 2. The "my eBay" interface offers a convenient button to relist non-sold store items.
  • 3. eBay's selling manager pro subscription enables *automatic* upgrading of 30 day listings to GTC.
  • 4. If Selling Manager Pro was ever installed on the account, and was subsequently removed, the automatic upgrading of 30 day listings remains in place. In order to disable the merchant must resubscribe to selling manager pro.
    Common issues related to this situation:
  • * Inventory reserves will expire the day the original 30 day listing was set to end. This will cause overselling!
  • * Syndicated listings which are automatically converted to GTC listings will NOT be able to checkout after 90 days of the original ending time.
    Many of the above problems/issues will also arise if you setup your ebay profiles to list store listings as GTC duration as well, so be forewarned.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I use multiple eBay usernames?
    Yes, when you launch a channel you can specify a username and password for that particular channel. Once saved into the channel it is permanent, the next channel you create can have a different username and password and will not affect the previously created channels.

    How can I change my eBay password?
    Click Here to Change your eBay Password

    I'm planning to change my eBay username, what can I do to make it go easier?
    eBay allows you to specify an email address instead of a nickname, use your registered email address in the username field with your regular eBay password and everything should go fine.

    How can I edit a running eBay Stores?
    Revoke the item and launch a new one.
