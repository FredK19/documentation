Syllabus: EBay 1A (Optional)

Ebay Account Setup Training

Integrating your Zoovy account with your eBay account is easy but with tons of versatility to sell in the manner you are used to. If you are an eBay seller, taking advantage of Zoovy to launch to eBay is usually a great idea. We can increase your upsell with items from your Zoovy webstore and add winning bidders to your newsletter subscriber list. Ultimately, we can bring the customers that we find off of eBay back to your Zoovy store rather than eBay. If you are not currently an eBay seller, you might want to consider this popular marketplace as another venue for customer acquisition.
This is an optional training, only required for customers that intend to use EBay as a marketplace.
Remember that customers who purchase from you in eBay checkout to your Zoovy store. Many Zoovy merchants use eBay solely as a customer acquisition tool.
EBay Account Setup Overview
  • Authorization Token
  • Profiles
  • HTML Wizard
    • You must have a correctly configured HTML Wizard and eBay Profile before any product can be launched onto eBay.

    Authorization Token

  • Click on the Setup Tab.
  • Click on eBay Configuration.
  • At the top, under the main tabs, click on eBay Accounts.
  • Click on Setup or Refresh eBay Authorization Token. This will take you to eBay and through a series of steps that will ask for your password. This is necessary to establish an authorization token for us to communicate with eBay on your behalf. note: if you get a "page cannot be displayed" error after clicking the 'I agree' button on ebay, your SSL and TLS encryption may not be enabled. Enable them in your Internet Explorer browser by going to Tools >Internet Option > Advanced and selecting the SSL and TLS options. Enable them in your Firefox browser by going to Tools >Option > Encryption and selecting the SSL and TLS options.

    EBay Account Setup Overview
  • Go to Setup
  • eBay Configuration
  • Enter you eBay user name
  • Select Profile and
  • Get Started

    • Profiles

    Once you have completed your Authorization Token, click on the profiles tab at the top of the screen. There are two parts of your Profile that you should configure before trying to launch to eBay - Config and Shipping.
    The Profile is essentially all the basic settings for your auction and store listings. Click on Config and go down the screen and configure each setting for how you sell on eBay.
    The profile you select will select the domain you would like your e bay customer to checkout out of.
    You can have as many different Profiles as you want for certain products that you might sell differently. Be sure to at least configure your DEFAULT Profile.
    Multiple User Ids on eBay
    While you can set up multiple user ids with Zoovy for selling on eBay, this may not be the best practice for your business. We suggest, rather, that the focus be more on your webstore and diversifying your business to other sales channels to become less focused on eBay, so that your business can grow.

    HTML Wizard

    The HTML Wizard refers to the look and feel of your eBay auction, fixed price auctions and store listings. You can have a different HTML Wizard for each of your eBay Profiles.
    Configuring an HTML Wizard
  • Go to Setup
  • Click on Company Profiles
  • Select the link under "eBay Template" for the proper Profile that you want to modify
  • Select an HTML Template
  • Modify the database fields that appear
  • Save and Preview your changes in the bottom window
    • eBay considers Google Checkout to be competition to PayPal. To avoid any possible conflicts with eBay you may not want to launch to eBay from the same site upon which you have Google Checkout.

    Homework

  • Before you move on to eBay 1B, ensure that you have at least one eBay Profile configured under Utilties > eBay Account Setup > Profiles. You cannot launch to eBay without a successfully configured eBay Profile.
  • Also, before you move on to eBay 1B, be certain that you have selected an HTML Wizard for eBay. Your HTML Wizard is found under Setup > Company Profiles. You cannot launch to eBay without a successfully configured HTML Wizard.
    If you require assistance with your homework, please schedule a training session at your convenience.
