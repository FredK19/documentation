Doc Overview

This page is dedicated to providing helpful tips when importing and exporting in Zoovy

Exporting Tips

Exporting Inventory
  • Step 1. Go to Reports
  • Step 2. Go to Inventory Reports
  • Step 3. Click on Export to CSV

    • Exporting Tips

    Exporting Products
  • Step 1. Go to Utilities.
  • Step 2. Go to Product Export.
  • Step 3. Click on Export to CSV.

    • If this is your first time or you have not received training on importing / exporting, please consult with support before doing any import. Any corruption to your database is billable by support to fix.
