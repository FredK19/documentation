Choosing Specialty Site Wrappers

How to change or choose a wrapper for a specialty site

To change the wrapper for a specialty site
  • Setup > Site Builder and click to expand the section named for your specialty site.
  • Click on Company Information and you can select the wrapper there.
  • If you dont see the one you desire in the dropdown, go back to Setup > Choose Theme and "remember" the theme you would like to see there. Only "remembered/favorite" themes show up in this dropdown.
