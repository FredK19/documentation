Zoovy Marketing Program

Concept Overview


Zoovy's marketing experts evaluate e-commerce marketing efforts to ensure that a business's budget is being distributed in the best possible areas. Our marketing program is designed to work with merchants with any size marketing budgets, in either an executive, implementation, or advisory role based on our clients needs.

Zoovy's marketing team will work with any business large or small and there are several different types of services we offer depending on what the business goals are:
  • Consulting - by way of a support ticket or appointment basis. Appointments are available through the online scheduler.
  • Flat Rate Packages (outlined below)
  • A la Carte: Hourly rate of $100/hr (link building, search engine optimization, PPC Management, marketplace assistance, etc.)
