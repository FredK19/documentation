How to Edit Your Policies


The policies that appear in a variety of places throughout your account are all found in the Company Information area. Your Company Information is edited under Setup > Site Builder.

How to Edit the Policy Pages

Shipping Policy, Payment Policy, Return Policy, Checkout Instructions, About, Contact Information

Your policies can be changed in one place.
How to edit your Company Information.
  • Click on Setup in the primary navigation.
  • Click on Site Builder.
  • Click on the Edit link adjacent to Company Information.

    • How to Edit the About Us Page

    This tells you how to edit the About Us page on your website.
    How to edit your About Us Page
  • Click on Setup in the primary navigation.
  • Click on Site Builder.
  • Click on the Edit link adjacent to About Us.

    • The About Us page goes by many names. Your theme may call it "Company" or "More Info," for example.

    How to edit your Returns Page
  • Click on Setup in the primary navigation.
  • Click on Site Builder.
