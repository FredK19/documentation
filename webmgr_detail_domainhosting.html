Modification
Any modification requests for domain ownership and contact information should go directly to Zoovy tech support. Please submit a ticket with any inquiries, or with Valid information to replace what is currently registered to the domain.
Only the registered owner of a domain may request contact changes

Privacy Settings

Zoovy does not offer Privacy settings for Hosted domains. The primary reason behind this is a Domain with private information is a large RED FLAG for ISP's, causing them to believe your email or newsletter correspondence may be spam. This can result in rejected email, bounced messages, or Black Listed Domains and service.
