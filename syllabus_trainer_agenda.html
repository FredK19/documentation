Training Syllabus: Training Overview

While the needs of every customer vary, these general topics are likely to apply to nearly every Zoovy user.
This overview is intended to be your guide through the Zoovy learning process. Go through the topics in the order they are presented. Review the syllabus list regularly for features that are available to Zoovy users. Those marked as "Optional" are not necessary for your success with Zoovy, but may assist you as your business grows.
If, after a training session, you find that you don't fully comprehend a topic listed on the syllabus, be certain to follow up by reviewing the Zoovydocs or scheduling another training appointment.
