Mobile Devices

Zoovymail for Mobile Devices

Many mobile devices can be configured to pick up your Zoovymail. The exact configuration will depend on the specific mobile device that you are using.

Blackberry

Many versions of Blackberry have an "E-mail Settings" icon. By clicking this icon you can send and receive Zoovymail from your Blackberry. For specific configuration, please consult the documentation for your mobile device.

iPhone

You use iTunes to sync your email accounts to iPhone. For specific configuration, please consult the documentation for your iPhone.

Motorola Q

The Motorola Q smartphones include an email application, so you can access your Zoovy mail/Fusemail while on the go. Doc 51037

Palm Treo

Some versions of the Treo include the VersaMail email application. You can set up Zoovymail in the VersaMail application on your computer and synchronize the information to your mobile device.

Other Devices

There are numerous mobile devices today that can be used to send and receive mail. Most of them have some protocol for configuring email that involves entering your user name, password and server settings.

Zoovymail has a URL intended for access by mobile devices at http://www.fusemail.com/mobile
As a last resort, many mobile devices integrate with Microsoft Outlook. We encourage the use of mail clients such as Thunderbird over Outlook but if you have no other recourse or prefer Outlook, it can be used as a middleman to bridge Zoovymail and your device.

The SMTP server for Zoovymail is smtp.fusemail.net
The IMAP server for Zoovymail is imap.fusemail.net

Zoovy does not officially support a specific mobile device. We can only provide the settings information for your Zoovymail account but for troubleshooting please contact your mobile device manufacturer or provider.
