Syllabus: Security and Trust (Optional)

Concept Overview

One way to increase sales and to start selling higher priced products is to increase consumer trust. This subject illustrates some methods by which you can provide a secure and professional web store.

HackerSafe

Doc 50871
Once you have setup a HackerSafe account, you may add a HackerSafe logo under Setup > Site Builder > Site Theme: Sidebar.

BuySafe

Doc 50732
BuySafe is configured under Setup > Analytics & Plugins > Buysafe Website Bonding.

Authorize.net Verified

Doc 50801
According to Authorize.net: "When you display the Verified Merchant Seal on your Web site, you increase your customersâ€™ confidence that their transactions are being processed securely by one of the most trusted names in the payments industry."
Authorize.net merchants can incorporate the Authorize.net Verified code and icon by going to Setup > Site Builder > Site Theme: Side Bar.

BBB Online

You can enter your BBB Online ID and add a BBB logo to your site by going to Setup > Site Builder > Site Theme: Side Bar.
You can sign up for the BBB Online at http://www.bbbonline.org/reliability/apply.asp

Twitter

You can place a Twitter logo on your website by going to Setup/Site Builder/Decals.

Skype

You can use Skype on your website by adding the logo to your website through Setup/Site Builder/Decals.
