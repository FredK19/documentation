Partitions

Partitions

Partitions allow for separate customer databases, checkout, payment methods, system messages and tax settings. You do need to partition all of these settings.
You may have limited access under certain Partitions. When you first log in, you will see "Partition Access" at top instead of "Master Access."
In order to utilize Partitions in your Zoovy account you must be granted rights by Zoovy Tech Support. It is not a feature that can be added through the Zoovy interface. The cost per partition is $50/mo. Contact the billing department if you have further questions about the cost of partitions.

Sites

Under the Sites tab, you can switch from one Partition to another. In parentheses next to each Partition you will see the customized settings that are unique for a given Partition. "0" indicates your original Partition.

Global Settings

Under Setup > Global Settings, you will find your Inventory settings. This is also where you can set your next order number.

Adding/Removing Partitions

Customers must use Zoovy Implementation Services to add partitions. Partitions are permanent and cannot be removed once added.
Zoovy System Operations can remove a partition for a $150 processing fee. This process normally takes 3-5 business days. To request the removal of a partition put in a support ticket.

You must configure settings on a partition (payment, shipping, etc.) before the domain assigned to the partition will function.
