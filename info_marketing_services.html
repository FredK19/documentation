Zoovy Marketing Services: Cookbook

Concept Overview

This document provides an overview of the list of tasks/job codes commonly used by marketing services, along with guidance about the scope of services and suggestions for properly estimating the hours on each type of job.
Recipes in this cookbook are broken into three categories: Basic, Standard, Advanced - reflecting the difficulty, and cost involved. In general every basic task should be performed before tackling standard tasks, and all applicable standard tasks should be done before beginning on advanced tasks.

Terminology

The following codes are used throughout this document to reference for estimating. SMALL-SITE: Small site - less than 10 products.
AVERAGE-SITE: Average Site - less than 12 top level categories, less than 1,000 sku's, less than 50 categories overall.
BIG-SITE: Big Site - anything bigger than an average site, quote varies significantly based on site size and "variances" throughout the site.
INITIAL-SETUP: the initial time "sunk cost" involved in researching and establishing the foundation for a marketing task.
WEEKLY-TUNING: the hours per week needed for ongoing maintenance and optimization. Actual services may be performed bi-weekly, or even tri-weekly as necessary, however the estimates are assumed to be every week. For example: 3 hours every two weeks, would be written as 1.5 hours every week.
MONTHLY-TUNING: the hours per month needed for ongoing maintenance and optimization.
QUARTERLY-REVIEW: the time that should be budgeted for an end of quarter report that is recommended to determine the effectiveness of the campaign and discuss ongoing options. Usually this will involve preparing a report and the time involved in presenting that report to the client. This is optional step, but is highly recommended.
TEAR-DOWN: removing creative assets from site after they have been deployed. tear downs are commonly associated with removing seasonal/holiday graphics for example.

Basic

Basic SEO

Check site meta tags, product descriptions, category and homepage content. Check alt tags on images. Offer suggestions about writing style and content. Review javascript on the site. Ensure Google site map is configured and enable webmaster tools.
Project Detail -- SMALL-SITE: 3 hours, AVERAGE-SITE: 6 hours, BIG-SITE: more than 6 hours.

Basic Adwords

Simple Keyword research (based on existing site search logs and seo tags). Establish 3-5 new campaigns. Includes Ad-copyrighting. Monitor and adjust keyword performance over period of time (typically 3 months).
Project Detail -- UPFRONT: 5 hours, WEEKLY-TUNING: 1 hour, QUARTERLY-REVIEW: 3 hours.
NOTE: Does not include account deposit or marketing spend costs.

Basic Newsletter

Ensure visibility of newsletter enrollment. Review past campaigns (if any). Discuss a simple campaign concept such as "new products", or "hot deals" with site owner. (No special coupon coding, or promotional are budgeted). Product selection and copyrighting in a system newsletter template (or using the clients existing newsletter template if they purchased one).
Project Detail -- UPFRONT: 6 hours, POST-REVIEW: 1 hour.

Syndication

Establish initial account. Category Mapping to Marketplace Taxonomy. Discuss and configure product exclusions. Verify Product Feed. Setup conversion tracking javascript, and integration with Google Analytics. Establish initial bids (if necessary).
Project Detail -- UPFRONT: 6 hours (1st site), UPFRONT: 4 hours (2nd site), MONTHLY-TUNING: 2 hours per site.
NOTE: Does not include account deposit or click through costs which vary per site.

Basic Banners

Work with the client to identify opportunities for creative assets to be deployed on the site. Create promotional campaign/setup coupons or discount codes. Deploy assets on site. Setup analytics and tracking of those assets and their effectiveness.

Standard Services

Seasonal Overhauls

Zoovy can develop and implement a set of creative assets for upcoming holidays. This typically includes a combination of banners, and a promotional newsletter. We recommend clients request a committed date well in advance of the holiday.
Project Detail -- UPFRONT: 10 hours, TEAR-DOWN: 2 hours, POST-REVIEW: 2 hours.

Speciality Sites

Discuss target/focus/goals, and product line(s) with client. Design Site, customize navigation, configure keywords, and build specialized content. Link into existing site with shared cart (if applicable).
Estimate varies with project size/scope usually 10-50 hours.

Back-in-Stock Message

The Notify-Customer when back in stock (P_INSTOCK message) is a powerful feature of Zoovy that must be integrated into the OUT_OF_STOCK message. Ideal for clients who have products which are routinely out of stock but which will come back in stock. Specific tasks: Customize out of stock message.
UPFRONT: 1 hour.

Customer Product Reviews

Establish strategy with client. Create incentive program around encourage customer product reviews. Develop follow-up marketing campaign to clients which have purchased to encourage reviews. Seed initial reviews from open third party content (e.g. forums with attribution). Integrate reviews into product layout if necessary. Configure and administer incentive program or integrate with existing rewards program. Establish and potentially administer review approval process.
UPFRONT: 3-15 hours + 1-10 hours of design.

Customized Analytics

Establish reporting goals. Work with design team to implement funnels, and click trail tracking throughout the site. Establish if A/B testing is appropriate and implement. Identify site elements which require click tracking. Verify operation of click tracking. Establishing checkout funnels. Customer behavior tagging. Tuning Analytics Javascript to track individual goals. Exclude merchant and Zoovy IP addresses to ensure accurate numbers.
UPFRONT: 10 hours minimum, tuning as needed to align with client goals.

Targeted SEO

Perform in-depth SEO assessment. Identify back-links, top competitive sites and applicability of products. Work closely with customer to establish SEO goals. Link building on 3rd party sites. Customizing product pages, category pages, homepage, and URL's for specific (targeted) keywords to influence results. Track ranking of keywords over time, as well as competitors.
UPFRONT: 25 hours, MONTHLY-TUNING: 10+ hours

Managed Syndication

Review existing syndication campaigns, regularly adjust bid-prices. Improve data mapping, and configure per-marketplace pricing if appropriate.
UPFRONT: 8 hours + ongoing maintenance based on frequency.

Press Releases

Establish strategy with client. Create "press release" category on site. Develop press release "company blurb". Configure press release template. Work with client to identify press-release worthy content. Create Press release content. Submit to PR+Newswire.
UPFRONT: 10 hours, 3-5 hours for each additional press release.

Multi-Currency

Analyze analytics to determine potential applicability. Implement currency calculator into a product layout to increase appeal to international buyers.
UPFRONT: 1 hour design/testing.

Advanced Services

Estimates on advanced services vary extensively based on customer requirements. Estimates provided are intended to be representative of a bare minimum.

Repricing Engine

Discuss applicability of developing a re-pricing engine, along with strategies and frequency for automated re-pricing to (for example) ensure client is always the lowest price and possible battle with other repricing engines. This could be done for both CPC, eBay, and Amazon.
Estimate: 15 hours development.

Rewards/Loyalty Program

Develop a unique rewards/point tracking (miles) system which equates dollars spent, or products purchased into incentive points. Develop conversion of points into gift cards (which can then be used for merchandise), or points into discount coupons, or points into dollars on a special site with limited inventory. Identify which items do not qualify for points, or which items might qualify for "bonus points" during special holiday timeframes, or on specifically qualified products. Determine if points can be used in conjunction with other coupons or promotions. Determine if pooling of points in a "team" is appropriate/applicable. Each rewards program must be developed uniquely to suite a business, it's products and customers. Estimate: 25 hours development.

Radio

Establishment of tracking domains, suggestions on applicable media buys based on customer demographics. Radio (especially syndicated talk shows) can be a powerful, inexpensive, and effective way to reach highly targeted customer segments across the country outside of traditionally highly competitive online markets, while also associating a company and it's products with an endorsement from a well known celebrity.

Television Commercials/Infomercials

Consult with client on the production of commercials, advertorials, or even full length infomercials. Tracking of traffic on a site to determine site conversion effectiveness. Television advertising and infomercials both involve an initial creative development cost, and then a series of ongoing media buys. Television also lends a certain degree of credibility to a product or company. This approach can work especially well for companies which have a physical retail counter in their local area as a way to solicit new customers.

Print Advertising

Consult with clients on the applicability of reaching targeted markets via. Similar to radio and television print offers a cost effective way to target niche segments of buyers who may be hard to find online. Print Advertising is especially well suited for companies which have products which are not searched for online.
