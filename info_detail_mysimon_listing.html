MySimon Syndication

Overview

Mysimon is a comparison shopping site for almost everything: Apparel, computers, electronics, jewelry, video games, and more. We gather prices on millions of products from thousands of stores, so you can compare products and find the lowest price before you buy.
Mysimon does not power their product listings

Effective Feb 2010: Non-tech listings are powered by Shopping.com. Tech listings are powered by CNET (Pricegrabber.com). For more information visit mysimon.com >> Help

Getting on MySimon

Merchants are not able to syndicate directly to MySimon. Zoovy merchants can get on Mysimon using the comparison shopping sites, Shopping.com or Pricegrabber, in syndication.
