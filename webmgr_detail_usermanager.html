Manage User Logins

Overview

Manage User Logins allows an administrative user to create sub-accounts for employees and specify which portions of this site each employee may access. Throughout this document the term "Master Account" or "Master User" refers to logging in to Zoovy without any specific user. The master account cannot be deleted, and always implicitly contains administrative priviledges. All User Accounts are created by first logging into the Master Account. The Master Account is always the same as the Zoovy store name. For example if the Zoovy store name is "ACMEINC" and is located at http://acmeinc.zoovy.com then the Master User is simply "ACMEINC".
If the Master User creates 3 separate employee logins for Phil, Bob, and Sharon then those logins would be "ACMEINC*PHIL", "ACMEINC*BOB", and "ACMEINC*SHARON" respectively. Each User account should have a different password than the Master Account. Access to the Master Account should be limited only to the business owner, or primary account contact for Zoovy. When adding a new user you can select which privileges that user needs to complete their job. Trusted employees can be given "Is Administrator" access (discussed later) so they do not need to login to the Master Account.

What is this and why did this change?

If there is one thing constant in business, it's change.

As the sophisitication of Zoovy has continued to advance, the profiles of businesses using Zoovy has changed as well. An overwhemling majority of Zoovy customers have multiple employees who access the Zoovy account on a daily basis. With this comes the need to track who is logging in, and what they are doing while they are logged in.

This feature will make both our customers, and Zoovy operate more efficiently. Here's what this does for you:
  • Accountability - under the old system, it was impossible to tell which employee made changes to things like inventory. This means tracking down mistakes after happened, and more importantly taking steps to prevent them in the future was pretty much impossible. If the employee who made the mistake can be easily identified so they can be made aware, and then properly trained as to how to use the system to prevent future issues - it will save customers hours of frustration trying to figure out how mistakes happen. Ultimately this translates into savings of thousands dollars in lost productivity by being able to more quickly identify mistakes and correct them.
  • Reduced Errors - as we've learned with our Enterprise customers, as soon as employees know that a detailed log is available of all their actions their productivity increases, and they also make fewer mistakes. As absurd as this sounds, it's seems everybody tends to doublecheck their work when they know any mistake will swifty and immediately end up back on their desk.
  • Better Support - Zoovy support currently spends more than half our day tracing down what eventually end up being determined are employee errors. The auditing makes it easier than ever to identify when changes happen and who made them - thereby assisting us in providing all customers with faster and more accurate support. This also means that Zoovy staff has more time to spend doing stuff that actually benefits you such as improving documentation, and adding features. Even if you don't have multiple users - this change will have an impact on you!
  • Reduced Exposure - Using the account manager you can restrict employees to specific areas of the site that are necessary to do their jobs, and nothing more. This reduces you and your customers exposure to threats from disgrunted employees. It also makes it easier to terminate employees because you only need to suspend their account - you don't need to change everybodies password.
  • Contractor Access - Do you have an SEO firm, or website designer who is working with you and needs temporary access to your site? You can set expirations on logins so you don't have to worry about removing them later. Plus since the expiration is displayed when they login, they know they better finish the project on time or else!
  • Increased Productivity - in the past, in multi-user configurations anytime somebody accidentally pressed the logout button everybody would be logged out -- this resulted in hours of lost work every week.
  • Enhanced Security - because only one person may access an account at the same time, Zoovy is now able to expire authentication tokens quicker (every 24 hours) thereby closing the gap where a person could attempt to fraudulently access your account. It also means that users can actually use the logout button which ensures if their computer or laptop is accidentally stolen, that the thief won't have access to their business.

    Planned Enhancements:

These multimedia training videos will be provided free of charge to all customers, and we hope they will dramatically reduce training costs for new employees.

    • Creating Users

    Creating users is easy, just go to Setup / My Account Settings / Manager User Logins. From there click the "Add New User" link, you will need to provide the following information:
  • Login Name: maximum 10 characters, consisting of letters A-Z and 0-9, the user will login STORE*LOGIN -- so if your Zoovy username is "ACME" and you create user "BOB" then on the zoovy homepage the login would be "ACME*BOB"
  • Password: create a password for the user, if you enter the password of "password", or leave the field blank then the user will be prompted to change the password after their first login.
  • Full Name: The individuals Full Name, or Nick Name this will eventually be displayed to customers when emails are sent by this user.
  • Job Title: This will be included as part of the upcoming "Signature" feature, and will also be included as a part of a Virtual Card for emails sent.
  • Email: This is the email address that you would like Zoovy to send email *FROM* and *TO* when this user is working in the account.
  • Phone: This phone number is included in the Virtual Card attachment in emails (so cellphones are probably a bad idea)
  • Is Administrator: This grants the user all the same priviledges as the Master Account, EXCEPT the ability to add/remove the Administrator flag from other accounts.
  • Is Customer Service: (NOTE: This feature is NOT YET IMPLEMENTED but will be relased in Q1 2005) Emails sent to the designated customer service email will be sent in a round robin fashion to an employee. The advantage of this is it means multiple customer service people don't need to share a single email address. You can configure this feature now, it will start working once the feature is released.
  • Expires: Enter a MM/DD/YY format date that the account should become inactive. Use this to give limited time access to contractors. DO NOT USE THIS TO TERMINATE EMPLOYEES - since the date the account will expire is displayed to the user when they login.
  • Setup Area:
    Grant Full Access Gives perpetual access to all Setup features, even new ones which are introduced later.
    Allow: Update Website Gives access to change categories, update meta tags, edit pages, and add/remove products from the website.
    Allow: Manage Images Gives access to list, add, update, and remove images. This is needed to access *any* images including company logos, and product images as well.
    Allow: Payment/Shipping/Checkout Gives access to most configuration settings throughout the system. The notable exceptions are the Account credit card area, and Billing History both which require Admin access.
  • Products:
    Grant Full Access Gives perpetual access to anything having to do with editing products (also gives access to manage images)
    Add/Update Gives access to add and edit products, along with inventory, edit options, and create store option groups. This does not give access to manage images.
    Remove Allows a user to delete a product.
    Create Channels Gives access to create channels (but not manage them).
    Syndication Setup Gives access to all syndication settings, and map categories.
  • Order Area:
    Grant Full Access Gives perpetual access to anything having to do with orders.
    Allow: View Orders Gives access to view orders and to update tracking numbers.
    Require Package Verification This will require that a user scan the proper product before proceeding to airbill printing in Order Manager.
    Allow: Add/Update Invoices Gives access to create new invoices, and update the properties of any invoice EXCEPT the payment status.
    Allow: View/Update Payment Detail Gives access to view credit card information, and to manually override payment status.
  • Manage/Reports:
    Grant Full Access Gives perpetual access to all management features.
    Allow: Reports Allows the user to view/access reports.
    Allow: Channel Management Allows the user to edit/cancel channels.
    Allow: Customer Management Provides access to the customer management features.
    Allow: CSV Import/Export Provides access to all import/export functionality.

    NOTE: throughout this document the word "perpetual" is means that as new features are introduced/changed over time, if the "Full Access" is checked, then the user will instantly have access to any new features added to that section.
