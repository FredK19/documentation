Site Builder - Gallery Page

Overview

The Zoovy Gallery provides a list of all auctions which are currently being tracked by Zoovy. You can edit the appearance of the Zoovy Gallery by going to Setup > Site Builder and clicking on the Edit link parallel to Gallery. If you would like to include a link to your gallery in your auctions you should link to http://username.zoovy.com/gallery.cgis (be sure to replace username with your username!)

Contents of the Gallery

Items will automatically appear in your Zoovy gallery page. The gallery will display all channels which are appropriate for a gallery view.

Display on a specific marketplaces listings

You can limit the results to a specific marketplace by linking to http://username.zoovy.com/gallery.cgis?marketplace=NAME where NAME is the name of a marketplace, such as ebay, ebaystore, bidfields, etc.

Why dont eBay channels appear?

You must enable the eBay gallery service. Search for the eBay Gallery Channel for more information.

Is there a charge for eBay Gallery?

The Zoovy Gallery service is free. The Zoovy eBay Gallery service is free. The eBay Gallery service (where eBay shows a picture of your item in the eBay.com search results) has fees associated with it, but those are charged by eBay - not Zoovy.
