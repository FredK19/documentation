Syllabus: Supply Chain (Optional)

Supply Chain Training

In this training, you will learn how to use Zoovy's Supply Chain Management feature.

What can supply chain do?

Supply chain encompasses a series of features which can be enabled individually or in combination:
  • Supply chain can email or fax an order to a supplier automatically.
  • Supply chain can place an order to a supplier on your behalf when your inventory reaches a certain threshold.
  • Supply chain can calculate shipping for specific products based on the location of that item.
  • Supply chain can use your supplier's inventory numbers to automatically update your products.

    • Suppliers

    You will first need to configure your suppliers under Utilities > Supply Chain Management.
    How to configure suppliers
  • Go to Utilities > Supply Chain Management and Add a Supplier.
  • Decide how you would like orders to go over to your supplier.
  • Associate the products that this supplier will provide.
    • Depending on the type of supply chain that you select, the details may change but all suppliers share these basic configuration steps in common.
    Only the specific Product ID's associated to a supplier will be effected by supply chain.
