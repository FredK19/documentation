Export Customer Information

Methods For Exporting Customers

This section will illustrate the different ways you can export your customer information

List all Customers

"List all Customers" Is a report you may generate from your Zoovy web interface that gives you a list of all your customers. This report does not include the customers physical address or purchase history. The information provided in this report is Email, Full name, if the customer has Opt In into your Newsletter & last Modified date.
This report should not be used to create a customer list for bulk or electronic newsletter mailing
Export the List all Customers Report
  • Step 1 Log into the web interface
  • Step 2 Go to Utilities Tab
  • Step 3 Click on Customer Manager
  • Step 4 On the blue horizontal line for reports click "List all Customers"
  • Step 5 This will generate the customer report
  • Step 6 Scroll to the bottom of the report
  • Step 7 In the "Jump to Page:" options please select "View All" in the drop down menu and click Go
  • Step 8 At the top of this window on the right hand side you will see a MS Excel Logo
  • Step 9 Click on the MS Excel Logo to commence your download of the customers


    • Newsletter Subscribers

    "Newsletter Subscribers" Is the report that will allow you to generate a list of all your customers that have OPT In to receive a electronic newsletter. This report does not include the customers physical address or purchase history. The information provided in this report is Email, Full name, Newsletter & last Modified date.
    If you plan on emailing your customers advertisements this is the only customer report you should use. These are the only customers that have opted in to receive a newsletter from you
    Export the Newsletter Subscribers Report
  • Step 1 Log into the web interface
  • Step 2 Go to Utilities Tab
  • Step 3 Click on Customer Manager
  • Step 4 On the blue horizontal line for reports click "Newsletter Subscribers"
  • Step 5 This will generate the customer report
  • Step 6 Scroll to the bottom of the report
  • Step 7 In the "Jump to Page:" options please select "View All" in the drop down menu and click Go
  • Step 8 At the top of this window on the right hand side you will see a MS Excel Logo
  • Step 9 Click on the MS Excel Logo to commence your download of the customers


    • Exporting Customer Information from Order Manager

    You have a more flexibility to what information you can export from your customers if you use the Zoovy Order Manager. This report will allow you to export the Full Name, Email, and Default Mailing Address
    This report should not be used to create a customer list for bulk or electronic newsletter mailing
    Exporting Customer Information using Order Manager
  • Step 1 Open your Zoovy Order Manager
  • Step 2 Click on "Customer Tool" button
  • Step 3 A new window popped up with your entire customer list
  • Step 4 Select the name at the top of your list
  • Step 5 Now scroll to the bottom of the list
  • Step 6 Hold the [Shift] key down on your keyboard
  • Step 7 Select the name at the very bottom of the list
  • Step 8 You will now see the entire list highlighted
  • Step 9 At the top of the window you will see a "Export" button
  • Step 10 Click on the Export button
  • Step 11 A new window will pop up, in this new window you will see two columns. The left column has Email, Full name, Total Count, Default Mailing Address. The column on the right is empty
  • Step 12 Now you need to select the fields you would like to export, by highlighting the field like "Email" as an example and click the arrow pointing to the right
  • Step 13 Whatever fields you have populated in the left column is what you will export
  • Step 14 Once you have finished selecting the fields you wish to export please click "OK"
  • Step 15 Now you will be prompted to save the file to your computer and you are done.
