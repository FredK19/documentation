Glossary of Terms

Zoovy to English

This is a handy Zoovy to English dictionary to assist you in communicating with the natives.

Glossary

API

An abbreviation for Application Protocol Interface. This usually refers to something Zoovy has custom built to perform a specific function.

Auction Template

An auction template is the look and feel for your EBay and/or OverStock listings. Also sometimes known as "HTML Wizard."

Channel

This is a data conduit between Zoovy and a marketplace. It is specifically used for auction sites such as eBay and OverStock which often require a variety of specific fields to be configured.

CWO

This is an abbreviation for "Custom Work Overview". If you have had custom design work done for you, the design staff may have uploaded a CWO files to your Custom Files Library. It is a PDF document that gives you instructions for how to implement your design.

CSV

CSV is an abbreviation for "Comma Separated Value." This is a type of file that is used to import data into Zoovy.

Domain

A domain is a web address. You might also know it as a "URL."

Exhibitor

A special Flash animation that is used with an RSS feed to display products over a period of time, usually tied to some kind of promotional price.

Favicon

A favicon (short for "favorites icon") is an icon associated with a particular webstore. Browsers that support favicons may display them in the browser's URL bar and next to the site's name in lists of bookmarks.

Gateway

A gateway or "payment gateway" is a way by which you accept credit card payments on your website.

Incomplete

The term "incomplete" is found under the Orders tab but it is a bit of a misnomer to call it an order. An incomplete is a checkout link that gives a specific customer a specific price. The most common use of incompletes is eBay where a bidder might win a particular item at a price that you would not normally sell it.

Inventory

In Zoovy, the word "inventory" usually refers to a numeric value representing how many of a specific product that you possess. This number is usually entered in the "On Shelf Quantity" box on the Product Editor page.

IPN

This is an abbreviation for "Instant Payment Notification." IPN delivers immediate notification and confirmation of PayPal payments you receive.

JEDI

"JEDI" stands for Just Easy Data Integration. It is a type of supply chain that shares information between two different Zoovy accounts. This kind of supply chain iwould be used if one Zoovy merchant is drop shipping for another Zoovy merchant.

Min Stock Qty

This is a trait used specifically with the Stock Supply Chain. It represents the inventory level at which the Zoovy supply chain will automatically re-order from your supplier.

Navigation Category

A page on your webstore. Sometimes shortened to "NavCats."

Package Verification

This means that the shipper must scan each product's UPC or SKU to verify the contents before printing out a FEDEX, UPS or Endicia Dazzle air bill.

Page Layout

This is where elements - such as products, text, and images - fall on a page. For example, if you wanted products to appear on a certain page, it would be a function of the page layout. Also sometimes called "Page Flow."

Partition

A partition allows you a different back end to your Zoovy account in order to accomplish some specific purpose. Some examples of why you may need a partition would be if you wanted to use different credit card gateways for different specialty sites, if you have more than one eBay account tied to your Zoovy account or if you wanted to maintain multiple customer databases.

PayPal EC

This is shorthand for PayPal Express Checkout.

Reserved

Reserved is a special inventory state used when an item is on eBay or OverStock. Reserved inventory cannot be purchased by other means but it is technically not out of stock until actually purchased.A unit of inventory is set aside for the listing. Should the listing end without a winning bid, reserved inventory is returned to it's in-stock status.

Root Level Category

A page on your webstore.

Shipping Rules

A set of special conditions to cause specific results in the way shipping is calculated for a customer. You do not need Shipping Rules to display or calculate shipping on your website.

SOHO Net

This is the flag that allows you to use multiple versions of Zoovy Order Manager. One version of Order Manager is considered the "SOHO" machine or the primary Order Manager that synchronizes with your online orders database. The other versions of Order Manager are considered "SOHO Clients." SOHO Clients modify the SOHO machine's database.

Swatch

A swatch is a color sample.

Subtitle

EBay's Subtitle allows you to insert an additional bit of text on your listings that is visible from the search page. You have 45 characters of additional text and additional fees apply. Note: Subtitles are searchable as part of a "Description Search" but not as part of a "Title Search".

Syndication

Syndication allows you to automatically send products to a marketplace. There are many different types of syndication. For example, eBay Store syndication, Amazon syndication, and Yahoo! Shopping syndication are all configured differently.

Theme

This is the overall look of your site. For example, whether your site is blue or red or any other color is a function of the theme. Also sometimes called the "Wrapper."

ZID

This is an abbreviation for Zoovy Integrated Desktop. It is one piece of software that includes both Zoovy Order Manager and Zoovy Warehouse Manager.

ZOM

This is an abbreviation for Zoovy Order Manager.

ZWM

This is an abbreviation for Zoovy Warehouse Manager.
