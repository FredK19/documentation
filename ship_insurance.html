Shipping Insurance

Overview

Unlike Handling the Shipping Insurance feature allows you to prompt the customer to optionally purchase insurance. Insurance can be computed in a variety of ways including based on the weight, the total price, the total weight, or based on a flat fee per item. Insurance is calculated independently of the shipping method (Zoovy assumes that the shipping methods being offered do not include insurance)

The rest of this feature is very self explanatory. Insurance fees can be in one of the following states:
Not Used/Disabled.
Included as part of the shipping total.
Displays as it's own line item under shipping.
In addition you may choose to make insurance either optional or mandatory.

Optional vs. Mandatory

If you use the Paypal Instant Payment button on the checout you must have your insurance as mandatory or customers paying via Paypal will NOT be prompted for insurance. This is not a feature currently supported by Paypal.

Insurance Mode: Flat Insurance Per Item

This mode allows you to differentiate between three destination zones: Domestic (50 states), Canada, and International. You can configure a price for a single item, and an optional surcharge for each additional item - this is the easiest way to go if all your products are similarly priced because it means you can easily make changes.

Insurance Mode: Per Product

This enables a special field in the web based product manager that lets you set a insurance amount per product. The insurance rate must be the same for domestic or international (although you can use this method in conjunction with other methods to add surcharges to international orders)

Insurance Mode: Weight Based

This mode allows you to differentiate between three destination zones: Domestic (50 states), Canada, and International. You may input a table of weights and prices.

Insurance Mode: Price Based

Similar to weight based method, the Price Based method allows you to ship to three zones, but the table works from the order subtotal.
When using price based, be certain to create an "empty cart" rule, which is if the total is $0.00, Insurance is $0.00. If you don't add this rule when someone empties the cart the previously quoted insurance amount will still display.

Insurance Mode: Special Rules

Using rules you can add special handling fees (or discounts) just like you would for any shipping rate. Rules are run at the end of the insurance calculation process once all enabled methods have been summed together. Using Rules it possible to add a surcharge as a percentage of the insurance total to select destinations.

Advanced Users

You can combine multiple insurance methods together to arrive at a composite total. The most common example of this is using the Flat Insurance per Item in Conjunction with the Per Product insurance mode. Since the Per Product mode does not give you the abilty to configure insurance based on a zone you can set a base insurance amount on a per item basis - and then add a surcharge for Canada or International destinations using the Flat Insurance Per Item.

Requirements

You much have the Shipping Bundle on your account to use this feature. You can add this bundle by going to Setup >> Add/Remove Features.
The "Shipping Bundle" includes:
  • Customer Pickup Shipping Method - allow customers in select zip codes to choose a local pickup.
  • Local Delivery Shipping Method - allow customers in select zip codes to opt for local delivery.
  • Shipping Rules - delivers the ability to fine tune shipping rates in a variety of ways using selective matching on products, zip codes, countries and more.
  • Handling Fees - create special handling fees which apply to all shipping methods.
  • Insurance Fees - allow customers to choose insurance, or make it mandatory for all orders. Insurance can be computed based on a flat per item fee, by the total price of the order, by the total weight of the order, or on a per product basis - ultimately you can also modify the final insurance result using shipping rules.
