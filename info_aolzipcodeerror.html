AOL ERROR: Mailing Zip is blank.

Symptom:

Receiving the following error:
"Warning: AOL ERROR: Mailing Zip is blank" (or similar)

Solution

Go to Setup > Site Builder > Company Info. Enter your Zip code and click on the Save button at the bottom of the screen.
A lack of Company Information can cause numerous problems throughout your Zoovy account. Please always be sure to take a few moments to fill out these few brief fields found under Setup > Site Builder > Company Information: Edit.
