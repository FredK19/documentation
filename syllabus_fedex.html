Syllabus: FedEx Labels Thru Zoovy (Optional)

FedEx Training

You can print FedEx airbills through Zoovy Order Manager.

PREREQUISITES

In order to print FedEx airbills you will need an FedEx account. You can obtain this account at www.fedex.com

Overview

  • Establishing connection to FedEx.
  • Tracking.
  • Void FedEx package.
  • Automatic emails.
    For international shipping, Commercial Invoice Print and Upload Flag submits electronically to FedEx. Additional labels or paperwork is not printed or necessary.

    • Homework

    When you have successfully printed out a FedEx airbill for an order through Zoovy Order Manager, you have completed this training topic.
