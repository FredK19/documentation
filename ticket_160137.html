Issue #160137: eBay Incomplete items not being created

We are having issues with eBay items selling and Zoovy not creating incomplete item orders.

Tech Response

In troubleshooting this issue, I discovered that your ebay authorization token is invalid. I don't know if your token expired, was cancelled, or removed from your eBay account but before I can troubleshoot this issue further, I'll need you to refresh your token.
This may well resolve the entire issue. If it does not, please update this ticket once you have refreshed your token.
Please include examples of eBay items that are not working.

Custom Question

Authorization tokens expire?? It was not cancelled nor revoked. How do I refresh it?

Resolution

I refreshed the authorization token by going to Manage | eBay | and selecting the "Accounts" tab, however the ebay items are still not showing in the incomplete file on Zoovy.

Resolution

The authorization token expires once per year. In addition, changing your ebay password or username can also cause it.
The listings won't create incomplete items immediately (automatically), you would need to click the "instant checkout" link in each one.
However the backup eBay polling system should catch anything which ended in the last week .. it could take a couple of hours.
In this case the backup polling system took about 15 minutes from the time the authorization token was refreshed.
