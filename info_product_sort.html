Sorting Products on Website

Concept Overview

This web doc will walk you through the steps of how to sort products on your website.
Click by Click Steps
  • Step 1 Click on setup > site builder
  • Step 2 Go to website navigational categories
  • Step 3 Click edit next to the desired category with products
  • Step 4 Go to product listing
  • Step 5 Click edit for product listing
  • Scroll to the top of the page
  • Step 6 Change the sort products by option

    • All options will be automated by the system except for Default / Manual Sort option.
