Syllabus: RSS Feeds (Optional)

Concept Overview

RSS Feeds can be used to easily share your product lists with affiliate sites and graphical displays.
One interesting use of an RSS feed is the Exhibitor. An Exhibitor is a Flash animation dynamically displaying time sensitive deals to your customers. Please contact your Business Development Representative for more details on how to get started with your Exhibitor.

How to Build an RSS Feed
  • Go to Setup > RSS Feeds
  • Click on the Create New tab at the top of the screen
  • Select the Domain/Profile to which you want this RSS Feed associated
  • Enter a Feed Campaign Code (This is a simple apha-numeric code to identify the RSS Feed in our system.)
  • Fill out the fields as needed.
  • Save.

    • If you apply a Wholesale Schedule to an RSS feed that schedule will apply to all products added to the cart, even those not in the RSS feed. For this reason, it is usually a good idea to create a unique Wholesale Schedule for your RSS feeds.
