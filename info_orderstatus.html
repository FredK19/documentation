Understanding Order Status

New (RECENT) Orders

When orders are created they are saved in the RECENT folder. While orders are in recent they can be cancelled by customers on the web.

PENDING and APPROVED

The statuses Pending, and Approved do not mean anything to Zoovy - they are strictly for your internal use. Some people place orders which are waiting for payment to Pending, and orders which have been paid for to Approved so they can be shipped.

COMPLETED

Once orders are shipped they should be placed in COMPLETED status. Orders in completed status will appear on sales reports, etc.

CANCEL

Once orders are placed into cancel then the inventory which was removed for those orders is added back to the available inventory.

ARCHIVE

Aftering finishing your yearly or quarterly taxes you can archive orders to reduce the number of orders displayed on the completed panels.

BACKORDER

The backorder tab is only available through the Zoovy Order Manager desktop client. It doesn't do anything special, other than give you another status like Pending and Approved which can mean whatever you want them to.
Order Manager also has another feature called "Sub Pools" that allows you to organize your data in a number of handy ways

Incomplete Items

Incomplete Items are NOT orders, orders have things like shipping and payment information which are not present in an incomplete item. If you flag an incomplete item as processed (completed) then it will it will disappear. The only way to find the incomplete item is to use the "show all" link. Processed Incomplete items are normally deleted from the system within 24 hours, unless they are placed on hold. You should not manually place incomplete items on hold, instead you should convert them to orders so they can be tracked and processed like orders. To convert an incomplete item to an order click the Incomplete button on the Orders tab - then check the box next to the incomplete item and finally click the "Create Order" radio button, then press "GO" - fill in all the known information, this will place the incomplete item as on hold until payment information is received. Remember that incomplete items are only a transitional phase between being a marketplace listing and a full fledged order.

Order Status: Archive

Archiving orders takes them out of the completed status, and hides them. This results in less memory consumption, and faster page loads. Archiving can also be explained simply as a hidden status, unlike recent, pending, approved, completed, cancelled which are visible statuses.

How to Archive Orders

Orders can be archived the following ways:
  • from Order Manager by right clicking on an order in the completed status
  • from the website by going to Orders / Completed and selecting an order
  • from the manage area by going to the Archive Orders

    • Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I archive a lot of invoices at once?
    Login to the website, go to Manage / Archive Orders. This will provides a fast and easy way to batch archive orders?

    Will archived orders appear in sales reports?
    Yes.

    Will archived orders appear in searches?
    Sometimes.
  • Archived orders will not appear on the Order Manager and website searches, but they will show up in searches on both systems if you search individually, using an order number.
  • Archived invoices will appear in the customer record.
  • Archived invoices will be included in data mining tasks in order manager.

    How can I recover an order that has been archived?
    There is no need to recover an order that is archived, because it is merely hidden. You can go to the website, search for the order #, and then from the invoice view change the status to another status.
