Syllabus: Site Builder

Site Builder Training

Site Builder is where you go to modify much of the content of your online store.
All the pages of your online store can be modified under Setup > Site Builder.
How to modify a page on your webstore
  • Click on Setup in the primary navigation.
  • Click on Site Builder.
  • Parallel to the page you would like to modify, click on the Edit link.
  • Find the section of the page that you would like to change and click on the Edit link next to that section.

    • Site Theme

    The Site Theme determines the overall look and feel for your online store. The font, colors, and buttons are all determined by the theme.

    Page Layouts

    A page layout tells Zoovy how you would like the content of that page to be displayed. You need to select a page layout the first time you modify any page.

    Product Listing Boxes

    Product Listing Boxes are special sections of a page in which you choose products to be displayed. You can also edit these boxes in order to select how those products will be shown (e.g., as plain text or with a picture).

    Homework

    Select and edit your theme and pages until you find a look and feel that you like for your webstore.
