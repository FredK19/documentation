Syllabus: QuickBooks Integration (Optional)

QuickBooks Integration Training

Zoovy Order Manager can conveniently and easily import your orders to your QuickBooks accounting software. You can use QuickBooks for completing your orders or for accounting purposes.

PREREQUISITES

You must complete basic Zoovy Order Manager training before taking this course.
For the most seamless integration, ensure that the Product ID's you used for your products in Zoovy also exist in QuickBooks.

Overview of ZOM Tabs

Be certain to back up QuickBooks before attempting the integration for the first time. This is always a good idea before making any changes in QuickBooks.
Three tabs in Zoovy Order Manager under Tools > QuickBooks Setup must be addressed in order to initiate QuickBooks integration:
  • General
  • QuickBooks 2006 and Higher
  • Product Creation
    You must have your Company File selected under the QuickBooks 2006 or Higher tab. Only version 2006 and higher are supported by Quickbooks, and therefore are the only version which can be supported by Zoovy.

    • Homework

    When you can successfully transfer an order to QuickBooks, you have completed this course.
