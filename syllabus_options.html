Syllabus: Options (Optional)

Options

This is advanced training for product creation that requires the use of options. It is not required for all Zoovy customers.
In Zoovy terminology, "options" refers to any choice the customer makes upon purchasing an item. For example: different sizes, colors, engraving, gift wrap, etc. If you do not need options on your products you may safely skip this training topic.

Types of Options

There are several different kinds of Options in Zoovy.
The way that you set up your options can affect the marketplaces that you syndicate in. For example, in order to get on Amazon you want to use inventorial Store Options.

Product Option Groups

Product Options are created in the product, under the Config Options button. Use product options when you've got a unique choice that only applies to one product. E.g. if the product UFO has one option group "Discomboluator" which has two options "Nuclear" and "Coal", and no other products in your store have Discombobulators then using a Product Option group is the best choice.

Store Option Groups

Store Options are built under Products > Store Option Groups. If you sell similar types of products then the majority of your options should be Store Option Groups.

System Wide Option Groups

System Wide Options are preconstructed options built by Zoovy for your convenience. They are found under Products > Store Option Groups.

Inventory

If you select "Yes," you will be tracking inventory by the different choices that are available for that option. For example, if you had a shirt that came in different sizes and you wanted to track how many of the small sizes you had versus how many of the large or extra-large, you would make the option effect Inventory.

Properties Only

This is used for a special kind of search. Do not check this box unless you know what you are doing.

Enable Assembly Processing

This is used for a special kind of option. Do not check this box unless you know what you are doing.

Options Tips

How to use Store Option Groups
  • Click on Products in the primary navigation at the top of your Zoovy account.
  • In the column on the left, click on Store Option Groups (next to the globe icon).
  • Click on the Create New button on the following screen.
  • Create the Store Option Group by making appropriate selections.
  • Go to the product that will have the Store Option Group on it.
  • Click on the Config Options button.
  • Click on the arrow next to the Store Option Group to add the Option to the product.
