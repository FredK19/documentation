Batch Channel Editor

Overview

The batch channel editor allows you to revoke, or delete multiple channels at the same time. Keep in mind that Deleting a Revoked channel will end the item on Ebay and award the item to the highest bidder.
Steps how to use Batch Channel Editor
  • Click on utilities
  • Click on Batch Channel Editor
  • Enable Batch Channel Revoke
  • Check the box to all channels you wish to revoke
  • Click on save
  • Enable Batch Channel Remove
  • Check the box of all the channels you just revoked
  • Click on save
    • Revoking a channel will prevent the channel from relisting. Removing / Deleting the channel will delete will end the item on eBay. Always revoke a channel before deleting the channel.
