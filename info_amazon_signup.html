Amazon Sign Up

Selling on Amazon

Give your products visibility and exposure to millions of buyers by selling on Amazon.com. Zoovy is proud to announce a special offer. Amazon Services is currently making available to Zoovy customers, one-month of subscription fees waived, a $39.99 value, for its Selling on Amazon service. There are no item listing fees, and Amazon will handle the payment processing. You just receive orders; ship them to the buyers and Amazon deposits net proceeds in your account every two weeks.
The standard subscription fee is $39.99 per month for Pro Merchant account. You must sign up for a Pro Merchant account rather than an Individual account. Individual accounts cannot be connected to Zoovy. By registering now, you will receive 1 month waived. Standard referral fees still apply, which are applied only when you sell a product.
You will need to add the Amazon Package to your Zoovy account in order to use our Amazon integration. You can add packages to your Zoovy account by visiting Setup > Add/Remove Features.

The Process

This is a fairly self-explanatory sign up process but there are a number of steps. To make it easier, we have outlined those steps here.
You will probably want to have certain information handy when you sign up: the credit card you will use to pay for your Amazon account, your phone, and a place to jot down the new password you will be generating for your Seller Central account. If you have your bank account information, you can expedite the first payment your receive from Amazon.

How to sign up for Amazon
  • Log into your Zoovy account.
  • Click on the Sign Up link beneath the Amazon icon on the Syndication page.
  • On the following screen, click on the link "Click here to Apply Now!"
  • Enter your email address. This is the Username/ID that you will use to log in at Seller Central (http://sellercentral.amazon.com) You might want to note it down so you don't forget it.
  • Enter your first and last name and create a password. Write down this password! You are going to need it later on to log in at Amazon Seller Central.
  • Enter "Your business's name, exactly as you want it displayed to customers on our site." This will be your Merchant Name that you will enter into Zoovy Amazon configuration. You will want to remember this as well.
  • Accept the terms of service and enter your payment information.
  • Enter your physical address.
  • Select a phone number where you can be reached. Amazon needs to verify your phone number. Ideally you will have this phone handy while filling out this application.
  • An autmated call will come to your phone and you will enter your PIN displayed on your computer screen into your telephone.
  • You will be asked to enter your Bank Account Information. You can do this later but to expedite your first payment you may want to have it handy.
  • At this point you will be brought immediately to your Seller Central account. Take a moment to get familiar with it. If you did not write down the password that you entered when you first created the account, we suggest that your first order of business be to change your password to something that you will remember. You can change the password under the "Settings" header to your left. When you come back to Seller Central, you will use the email address that you entered at first and the password that you have established here.
    • You will log in to your Amazon account at sellercentral.amazon.com
