Western Union Bidpay

Overview

BidPay is available under payment methods (Setup > Payment Methods > Special) as a payment type, however, we are unable to reconcile when you receive money.
BidPay does not offer any sort of integration for companies like Zoovy (they only work with auction sites, not with e-commerce storefonts).
Bidpay requires a large amount effort to reconcile payments manually, and will almost certainly hurt your sales.
