Syllabus: EBay 1B (Optional)


Setup > Ebay Configuration


This is the intermediate training for launching to eBay. In this session, you will learn how to use the settings located in the Sub-Tabs under Setup > Ebay Configuration to configure your store for optimum Ebay listing performance.

Ebay Account Sub-Tab

You should be familiar with some setting on this sub-tab after setting up your Ebay account. If your token is active, you will see a box here titled "Active Accounts:." Inside this box, you will find the following information and functions:

Username:
Your Ebay account that is connected to this partition. You are limited to one Ebay account per partition.
Token Expires:
The date your token connecting this account to Zoovy is set to expire.
Checkout:
Partition and domain information for the ebay account.
[Deactivate]:
Removes the Zoovy connection from this ebay account.
[Refresh Store Categories]:
If your ebay account is a store account with store categories, you can pull these categories into zoovy with this button. The categories can then be set by drop down menu on the product's Ebay 2.0 panel under "eBay Store Category:"
[Refresh Authorization Token]:
Refreshes authorization token.
Setup or Refresh eBay Authorization Token:
Refreshes authorization token.
