Welcome to Zoovy!

Congratulations and welcome to Zoovy!
This brief introduction should help you build your Zoovy account, but it is by no means intended to replace the usual training process. Please be certain to schedule a training session as soon as you've decided to go with Zoovy. To schedule training, click on the Support link in the upper right corner of your Zoovy account. On the next screen, click on the Schedule Appointment link, next to the picture of the mouse. The times that appear are available appointments. Click on the slot that is convenient to you. Your personal business consultant / trainer / implementation assistant / project manager is waiting for you.
Even if an appointment is not available for several days, you are not “dead in the water.” Please take the time to go over the following before you enter training.

Shipping

Since you are building an online store, it's traditional to charge a customer for shipping upon checkout. You will need to ship the item after it has been purchased, of course, but what we are concerned with right now is how the customer is charged for shipping. Some things to consider…
  • If you have been selling online previously, how do you currently charge for shipping?
  • If you want to charge actual rate based on the weight of the item and the location it is being shipped to, whose rates should you use? UPS? USPS? FedEx? DHL? If you do not already have one, you may want to open an account with your preferred shipping provider.
  • Do you know how you will calculate shipping? If so, great! You may proceed. If your shipping is “complicated,” take time to figure out what makes it so. If you understand the stumbling blocks in configuring your shipping, your trainer may help come up with a solution. (Of course, first we need to diagnose the problem)
    • The Shipping setup is found in your Zoovy account under Setup > Shipping. If you feel comfortable configuring this on your own, feel free to do so, but your trainer will be happy to assist you.

    Payment Methods

    Please take a moment to consider how you want to accept payments. Some things to consider in advanced might be…
  • Will you take personal checks? If yes, to whom will they be made out?
  • Will accept credit cards? If yes, how will you process them?
  • Will you accept PayPal?
    • The Payment Methods setup is found in your Zoovy account under Setup > Payment Methods. If you feel comfortable configuring this on your own, feel free to do so, but your trainer will be happy to assist you.

    Inventory

    Please take a moment to consider how you want to track inventory. Some things to consider in advance might be…
  • Is inventory an issue for you?
  • Will you be tracking your inventory through your Zoovy account?
    • The Inventory setup is found in your Zoovy account under Setup > Inventory. If you feel comfortable configuring this on your own, feel free to do so, but your trainer will be happy to assist you.

    Product Creation

    If you are going to run an online store, you are going to need something to sell! You can begin creating your products before you enter your first training session. To create a new product....
  • Log into your Zoovy account.
  • Click on the Products tab in the primary navigation at the top of the screen.
  • Click on the New Product link.
  • Fill out the Add Product screen.
  • Save. You have just created a product!

    • Choose Theme

    Your theme is the look and feel of your online store. You can choose a theme before you enter your first training session. To choose a theme....
  • Log into your Zoovy account.
  • Click on the Setup tab in the primary navigation at the top of the screen.
  • Click on Choose Theme.
  • On the left, you will see a variety of methods for finding a theme. Start in Community Favorites and work your way down. The Preview link beneath each theme lets you see what it will look like on your store.
  • When you find your theme, click on the Select link beneath it.
