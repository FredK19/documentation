Syllabus: Shipping Rules (Optional)

Shipping Rules Training

This is an optional training session required only for Zoovy store owners with special shipping needs.
Shipping rules create an exception to how your shipping normally behaves. It is therefore important to thoroughly test your shipping rules to catch any unexpected behavior before your customer does.

PREREQUISITES

Before going into training, have a clear idea of how you would like to structure your shipping. You may need to write it out on a piece of paper or even construct a chart in order to clarify your specific shipping scenario.

Rule Name

Schedule

This is for users of the Wholesale Feature set only. If you wish to associate the shipping rule to a particular Wholesale Pricing Schedule, you may do so here.

Homework

Test your rules thoroughly by checking out from your online store as if you were a customer. Try to catch any possible problems before the customer does.
