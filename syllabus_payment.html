Training Syllabus: Payment Options

Overview

You can enable/disable select payment options for your store. These settings are global and cannot, through the standard interface, be edited per profile.

General Payment Options

Make Checks, Money Order, etc. Payable to:

The information in this field is used during checkout to let your customer know who their checks should be made out to.

Payment Options

There are several different types of payment options available that you can enable for domestic, safe international countries and all international countries. Some of these payment types allow you to add a special handling fee which will be applied to the order and appear on the invoice.
Wire transfer also allows you to display special instructions to your customer once that method has been selected during checkout.
Check on Delivery and Cash on Delivery are "C.O.D." Many customers do not realize this means C.O.D. until you actually say it. Make certain the student understands what it is he or she is enabling.

Credit Cards

Setup Authorize.net/e-Online Data
  • Click on Setup in the primary navigation.
  • Click on the Payment Methods link in the middle Store Properties box.
  • Click on the Credit Cards tab in the top navigation.
  • Select "Authorize.Net / eCheck" and "Proceed."
  • On the following screen, there is a hyperlink labeled "Configure Username/Password." This is NOT the username and password used to log into authorize.net. It is the Gateway ID and Transaction Key generated after the customer has logged into there authorize.net account.
