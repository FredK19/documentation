JEDI Reseller List

JEDI Suppliers and Retailers

This is a list for Zoovy merchants looking to act as suppliers to other Zoovy merchants. The Zoovy JEDI integration allows Zoovy merchants a quick easy way to augment your inventory.

The JEDI Reseller List

It is free to be listed here but you must meet certain criteria.
Requirements to be listed
  • At least one year with Zoovy.
  • You must have the Supply Chain function on your Zoovy account.
  • Submit a Support ticket requesting to be added to the list. The subject should be "Add (username) to JEDI Reseller List."
  • Give us your Zoovy username and a description of no more than 200 characters. (Note: We will not spell check your work.)
  • Also include the URL that you want published to the JEDI List. This domain must be hosted by Zoovy.
