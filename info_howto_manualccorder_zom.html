Creating Credit Card Order Manually in 6.x ZOM

Overview

This webdoc covers the steps required to create a credit card order in Zoovy Order Manager (ZOM) versions 6.x. It also briefly covers how to adjust the payment of credit card orders.

Creating Credit Card Order Manually

Create the order
  • Step 1 - (In Order Manager) click the "Create Invoice" button
  • Step 2 - Add customer information, or lookup customer withthe "Search" button
  • Step 3 - Add product by clicking the "Add Item" button
    • Set Payment Type
  • Step 4 - Click on the "Payment" tab
  • Step 5 - Choose payment type from dropdown (CREDIT)
  • Step 6 - Enter credit card information (exp. date, CCV/CID, card number)
  • Step 7 - Click on the "Payment" button, top of window
  • Step 8 - Set radio button to "Charge", confirm charge amount, and click "Charge Credit Card"
  • Step 9 - Save and Exit, order will appear in "Pending" folder w/ the status of "Paid" (assuming the charge went through correctly)

    • Changing Payment Status

    Suppose the was a problem with the order and you needed to adjust the payment status (from PAID to PENDING, PENDING to PAID,DENIED to PAID, etc....). You cannot right click the order and use the "Change Payment To" interface for credit card orders. Instead, follow the steps below.
    Change Credit Card Order Payment Status
  • Step 1 - (In Order Manager) right click order, select "Payment"
  • Step 2 - Review and if needed change the payment/credit card info
  • Step 3 - Click the Credit Card Gateway tab
  • Step 4 - Click "Change Manaually", this will open a new tab "Manual Credit Card"
  • Step 5 - in the new tab, click the new status you desire, and then save changes.
