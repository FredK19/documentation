[[SECTION]Setup Overview]

[[CBC]How to do a basic PPC syndication]

  •  1. Setup an account with the proper marketplace (e.g., Nextag, Google Merchant, etc).

  •  2. Click on Syndication and the corresponding icon to the marketplace to which you would like to syndicate.

  •  3. Enter your account information for that marketplace into the field provided.

  •  4. If necessary, map your online store categories to corresponding marketplace categories.

  •  5. Save.
[[/CBC]]
[[BREAK]]
[[HINT]]
Products must be set to active in order to syndicate into the feed
[[/HINT]]

[[CAUTION]]
Each shopping engine requires specific data, so once you have published the feed make sure to check the Zoovy interface for any products with errors.  It is also recommended to check the shopping engine after 24hrs to see if you products have went live
[[/CAUTION]]
