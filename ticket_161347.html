Issue

Issue: cod payment is disabled but customer selected cod
My COD payment is disabled but I had a customer select COD in order order # 2007-11-XXXX? I do not accept cod. I checked payment methods and cod was disabled can someone help me please.

Resolution

There are two types of COD in the zoovy system:
  • Cash/Money Order on Delivery
  • Check on Delivery check to make sure that you have both of these types of payment methods disabled. To do so go to: Setup > Payment Methods
